CONTEXT: MICROSOFT STACK (For Your Reference)
----------------------------------------------

Sentinel: SIEM/SOAR platform. Aggregates alerts, triggers playbooks. NOT a content detection tool.

Purview DLP: The actual guardrail layer. Detects sensitive content based on Sensitive Information Types (SITs), sensitivity labels. Can block Copilot from processing labeled content. Prompt-based SIT detection is in preview, GA expected late March 2026.

Azure AI Content Safety / Prompt Shields: Real-time detection of prompt injection, jailbreak attempts, harmful content. Model-layer protection.

Defender for Cloud Apps: CASB functionality. Visibility into shadow AI/SaaS usage.

Key insight: If Lam is relying on Sentinel as their primary guardrail, that's an architecture misunderstanding. Sentinel alerts on events - it doesn't block content.

CONTEXT: GUARDRAIL TESTING (For Your Reference)
-----------------------------------------------

Key metric: Attack Success Rate (ASR) - percentage of successful attacks over total attempts

"20% error rate" likely means:
- False negatives: 20% of sensitive content getting through (most dangerous)
- Or false positives: 20% of legitimate content blocked (productivity impact)
- Or combined rate - need to clarify

Red teaming tools exist (Microsoft PyRIT, Nvidia Garak, F5 AI Red Team) but many enterprises don't use them systematically. Testing methodology matters as much as the guardrails themselves.
