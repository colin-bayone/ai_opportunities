05 BayOne's Approach

Hybrid Detection Architecture

A proven approach from similar environments, including semiconductor equipment companies with defense and export control constraints.

Deterministic Layer

For known, enumerable entities, deterministic matching provides the fastest and most accurate detection. Zero false positives on known patterns because it is exact matching against curated dictionaries, not probabilistic AI.

Entity lookup tables for customer names, fab identifiers, and their known spelling variations
Pattern matching for structured identifiers with predictable formats
Maintained as configuration, not model weights — updated through standard tooling
Enterprise tooling: Microsoft Purview with custom Sensitive Information Types designed for domain-specific entities.

AI Classification Layer

For content where deterministic matching cannot reach, AI-based classification provides contextual analysis of unstructured and ambiguous content.

Contextual sensitivity: determining whether a mention is sensitive in its specific context
Unstructured text analysis for freeform descriptions and transcripts
Document-level classification for overall risk assessment
Enterprise tooling: Azure AI Foundry for scalability, manageability, and integration with enterprise identity and compliance controls.
