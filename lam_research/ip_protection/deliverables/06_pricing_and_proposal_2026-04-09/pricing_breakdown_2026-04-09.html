Pricing Basis

This engagement is priced at $15,000 based on the current understanding of the Escalation Solver application, the two target entity types, and the five free-text fields as described during discovery meetings. Scope is based on a representative data set agreed during the discovery phase. The pricing reflects a fixed-price, outcome-based model. Pricing is the same for both execution options (Lam Research environment or BayOne environment) as described in the Engagement Pricing document.

During the discovery phase, BayOne will gain firsthand understanding of the data landscape and detection complexity. If that analysis reveals opportunities to streamline delivery, BayOne will share those findings proactively. Any material changes to scope would be addressed through mutual agreement.

Scope Boundaries

This pricing covers detection of two entity types (customer name, fab identifier) across five free-text fields within Escalation Solver. The following are excluded from this engagement: additional entity types, additional applications, production integration, document and attachment scanning, and ground truth remediation at scale. Requests beyond these boundaries may require a change request with adjusted pricing and timeline.
