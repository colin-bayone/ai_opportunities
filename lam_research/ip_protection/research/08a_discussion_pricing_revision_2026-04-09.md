## Decision: $15,000

Colin confirmed $15K as the final price. The logic:

- $10K is underwater on loaded cost. Not viable.
- $12.5K gives only 13.5% loaded margin. Below the 30% floor and leaves no negotiation room.
- $15K gives 27.9% loaded margin. Slightly below the 30% floor on loaded, but 39% on base+travel. If the risk reserve is not consumed, effective margin is 39%.
- $15K is a clean round number. It is still well within reasonable range for a three-week engagement solving a problem Lam could not fix in 18 months.
- $15K gives negotiation room. If Brad pushes back, there is space to come down to $12.5K and still be above breakeven on loaded cost. At $10K original anchor, there was no room at all with the revised cost basis.
- The team (Anuj, Amit) both independently pushed for $12.5K-$15K in Set 08. $15K aligns with their instinct.
