Lam is a $17B company with sophisticated engineers but no AI expertise. Cloud architecture, RBAC, unified control plane, broader AI enablement are all natural next phases. This mirrors what Deloitte and Capgemini have done at Lam for years.

The debrief is high-energy and borders on dismissive of Lam's technical capabilities. While the underlying assessment (Lam lacks AI expertise, prior approaches were wrong) is accurate, the tone needs significant calibration before any of this thinking informs client-facing materials. Brad explicitly warned against being sold to. Overconfidence in delivery is the fastest way to trigger his vendor scar tissue.
