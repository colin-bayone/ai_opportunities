## Why This Change

Lam Research has elected the Lam Research environment as the execution mode. Presenting the SOW with two options (Option A vs. Option B) and labeling the chosen one as "elected" is redundant and signals that an alternative was on the table. The cleaner posture is to describe the execution environment as a fact of the engagement, not as a selected option. There is only one execution environment in this SOW.

---

## What To Do

Two edits, both within Section 1 (Services to be Provided).

### Edit 1: Replace the "Execution Options" Subsection

**LOCATE** the subsection currently titled **Execution Options** within Section 1. It contains a comparison table with two columns (Option A: Lam Research Environment elected; Option B: BayOne Environment) plus a closing sentence about the three-week period of performance.

**DELETE** the entire subsection: the header, the comparison table, and the closing sentence beneath the table.

**REPLACE** with the following subsection:

Header: **Execution Environment**

> The engagement is executed within the Lam Research environment. Lam Research data remains within Lam Research systems at all times. Contractor requires environment access (such as a virtual machine or equivalent), access to Escalation Solver data, access to reference materials, and equipment as agreed between Contractor and Lam Research IT prior to the engagement start date. Project leadership is provided by the Director of AI, BayOne Solutions.
>
> The three-week period of performance begins from the date all required environment access, data access, equipment, reference materials, and Lam Research subject matter expert availability are in place.

Formatting: Match the body paragraph styling used elsewhere in Section 1. Bold the subsection header **Execution Environment**. No table, no bullets, no comparison framing.
