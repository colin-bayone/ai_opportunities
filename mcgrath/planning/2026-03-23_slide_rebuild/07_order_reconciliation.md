## Key Differences and Whether They're Justified

### 1. NEW "Our Understanding" section (slides 04-07) -- NOT in original

**Original:** Solution divider at position 5, Scope Summary at position 6. Get to the solution fast.

**My order:** "Our Understanding" divider, Architecture, 13 Towers, Scope Summary (4 slides before the solution starts).

**The tension:** The original proposal's #1 insight was "Too Much 'About Us' Before the Solution." It compressed the intro to 4 slides and hit the solution by slide 5. My order adds 4 "understanding" slides, pushing the solution to slide 8.

**Is this justified?** Partially. The Architecture and 13 Towers are about McGRATH, not about BayOne. They demonstrate technical depth and understanding. But 4 slides of "we understand you" before "here's what we'll do" might be too much.

**POSSIBLE FIX:** Drop the "Our Understanding" divider (slide 04). Architecture + 13 Towers + Scope flows naturally without a divider. That saves one slide and gets to the solution by slide 7.

**OR:** Move 13 Towers and Scope to AFTER the solution section (as appendix/reference material) and keep only Architecture in the understanding block. Solution starts at slide 6.

### 2. Enterprise AI + Quality Engineering in Solution section -- SUPPORTED by original

**Original:** Flagged Ariat 11 (Enterprise AI) as "Should consider adding" and Ariat 12 (Quality Engineering) as "Worth reviewing." Both went after the AI Strategy slide.

**My order:** AI Strategy (10), Enterprise AI (11), Quality Engineering (12). Three AI slides in a row.

**Is this justified?** Yes -- Colin approved both. But three consecutive AI slides might be overkill. The original's concern was that "having only one AI-focused slide may not be enough." Two AI slides (Strategy + Enterprise) might be the sweet spot, with QE moved elsewhere or cut.
