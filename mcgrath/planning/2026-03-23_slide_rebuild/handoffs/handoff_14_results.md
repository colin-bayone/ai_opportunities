## Slide 20: Key Asks (Deduplicated)

**Source:** Old slide 28 (Final), with approved rewrite
**Design:** 2x2 card grid with gradient top-bar accents, icon headers, and descriptive paragraph text. Intentionally different layout from slides 18-19 (cards vs. table) to visually distinguish "asks" from "risks."

### Deduplication Details

**Problem:** All 4 rows in the original Key Asks slide were verbatim copies of the first 4 risks in slide 18. With these slides now at consecutive positions (18, 19, 20), every row was a visible repeat.

**Solution (approved by Colin):** Rewrote all 4 asks to focus on concrete, actionable requests rather than restating risk framing.

| Original Title | New Title | Change |
|---|---|---|
| Facilitation of KT & Documentation availability | Knowledge Transfer Facilitation | Action-oriented, shortened |
| Infrastructure support | Environment & Infrastructure Access | Specific about what's needed |
| Information | Timely Information Sharing | Adds timing expectation |
| Sign-offs, Approvals | Approvals & Decision Cadence | Frames as workflow commitment |

**Key principle applied:** Slide 18 says "this could be a problem." Slide 20 says "here's what we need you to do about it." Same four topics, completely different angle with specific asks (timelines, named roles, defined workflows).

**Icons used:**
- Knowledge Transfer: `fa-users`
- Environment Access: `fa-key`
- Information Sharing: `fa-share-nodes`
- Approvals: `fa-stamp`
