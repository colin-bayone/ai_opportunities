## Questions Potentially Too Granular (4)

### Q17 — Skill Certifications

> "For dedicated resources, what skill certifications are required (Oracle Fusion certified, Salesforce Admin certified)? Any that are preferred but not required?"

**Concern:** Asking for specific certifications by name signals we're worried about staffing qualifications. Experienced MSPs know certification requirements emerge during contract negotiation, not Q&A.

**Recommendation:** Simplify

**Suggested revision:**
> "For dedicated resources, are there specific skill certifications or experience requirements that MGRC considers mandatory vs. preferred?"

*Removes the example certifications, which makes us look like we're already checking boxes.*

---

### Q19 — Change Management / CAB Processes

> "Does MGRC have existing change management policies, CAB processes, and maintenance window schedules for the MSP to adopt? Or should the MSP build these in Phase 1?"

**Concern:** "CAB processes" is enterprise IT jargon that might not land well with MGRC's team. Also slightly detailed — we're asking about three separate things (policies, CAB, maintenance windows) in one question.

**Recommendation:** Keep as-is (borderline)

**Rationale:** This is a legitimate scoping question. The detail helps clarify what we're asking. If simplifying, combine concepts:

**Optional revision:**
> "Does MGRC have existing change management policies and maintenance schedules for the MSP to adopt, or should we propose a governance framework during Phase 1?"

---

### Q26 — PCI Compliance Specifics

> "For the Customer Hub (nopCommerce), can MGRC confirm the PCI compliance scope (SAQ type), pen testing frequency, and whether the MSP maintains PCI certification or supports MGRC's audit?"

**Concern:** Asking for "SAQ type" is auditor-level detail that MGRC may not know offhand. "Pen testing frequency" is due diligence material. This question packs three distinct asks that feel like a compliance checklist.

**Recommendation:** Simplify

**Suggested revision:**
> "For the Customer Hub (nopCommerce), can MGRC clarify the PCI compliance scope and whether the MSP is expected to maintain PCI certification or support MGRC's existing audit process?"

*Removes SAQ type and pen testing frequency — those come during due diligence.*

---

### Q27 — Security Monitoring Specifics

> "Does MGRC have existing SIEM/SOC capabilities, or will the MSP provide security monitoring and incident response? What is the frequency of vulnerability scanning and pen testing?"

**Concern:** "Frequency of vulnerability scanning and pen testing" is audit-level detail. Combined with Q26, asking about pen testing twice signals anxiety about security scope.

**Recommendation:** Simplify

**Suggested revision:**
> "Does MGRC have existing SIEM/SOC capabilities, or will the MSP provide security monitoring and incident response?"

*Removes the frequency question entirely — it's not needed for pricing and adds to the pattern of security anxiety.*
