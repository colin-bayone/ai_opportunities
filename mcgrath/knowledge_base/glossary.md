## Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Big Bang go-live | Launching all systems at once on a single date |
| Bridge letter | Document covering compliance gap between audit report date and current date |
| Hypercare | Intensive support period immediately after go-live |
| Phased go-live | Launching systems in waves over time |
| Rebadge | Transferring existing contractor staff to new employer |
| Shared Responsibility | Work split between MGRC and MSP (most common model in this RFP) |
| Tribal knowledge | Undocumented expertise held by individuals |
