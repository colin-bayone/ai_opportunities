## Ariat GCC Recommendations

### Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Foundation
- Select onboarding platform (Enboarder or SAP SuccessFactors)
- Deploy knowledge management (Glean or Guru)
- Begin expert knowledge capture from founding team
- Create initial learning paths

### Phase 2 (Months 4-9): Scaling
- Refine personalized learning paths
- Expand knowledge base with tribal knowledge
- Scale mentor matching with AI coordination
- Add compliance training automation

### Phase 3 (Months 10-18): Optimization
- Self-healing knowledge base
- Advanced predictive retention analytics
- Full administrative automation

### Budget Estimate
- **Platform costs:** $69,000-$220,000 annually
