## GCC Culture Building

### Key Success Factors
- Deploy HQ leaders early to embed culture
- Avoid silos - unified workflows with headquarters
- Balance global values with local practices
- Hire local managers who understand both contexts

### Scaling 30 to 200 Employees

**Phase 1 (Months 0-6, 30-60 people):**
- Implement pulse survey platform
- Deploy values-tied recognition
- Establish baseline metrics
- Train managers on AI dashboard interpretation

**Phase 2 (Months 6-12, 60-120 people):**
- Full culture analytics platform
- Predictive attrition models
- Virtual team-building activities
- Lifecycle surveys at onboarding milestones

**Phase 3 (Months 12-18, 120-200 people):**
- Self-healing culture feedback loops
- Advanced analytics and benchmarking
- Values alignment visible across organization
