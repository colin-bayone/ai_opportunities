# Key Findings Summary

## Market Adoption
- **43% of organizations** using AI for HR/recruiting (2025), up from 26% (2024)
- **Gartner predicts 70%** of large organizations will use AI for recruiting by 2026
- **Only 1.4%** qualify as fully "AI-First" with mature governance

## India GCC Context
- India hosts **1,700+ GCCs**, nearly half of world's total
- Expected to create **4.25-4.5 lakh jobs in 2025**, 1 million by 2030
- GCCs moving toward **agentic AI framework** for autonomous interview workflows

## Top Case Studies

### Unilever
- **90% reduction** in time-to-hire (4 months → 4 weeks)
- **£1 million** annual savings
- **16% increase in diversity**
- **50,000 hours** saved by recruiters
- Tools: Pymetrics, HireVue

### IBM Watson Recruitment
- **30% decrease** in recruitment costs
- **27% increase** in interview rate
- **95% accuracy** in attrition prediction
- **30% reduction** in bias

### Enterprise Home Care Provider
- **296,000 candidate screens**
- **138,000 interviews** scheduled
- **148,000 recruiter hours** saved (~$3.29M value)

### Paradox (Olivia Chatbot)
- **32 million interviews/year** scheduled
- Used by McDonald's, FedEx, 7-Eleven
- **75%+ reduction** in time-to-hire
- Workday acquired for **$1 billion** (Oct 2025)

## Quantified Outcomes

### Time Savings
- **33% reduction** in time-to-hire and cost-per-hire
- **340% ROI** within 18 months
- **52% faster** time-to-fill (42 days → 20 days)
- **70% reduction** in manual screening time

### Cost Savings
- **40% cost-per-hire reduction** for mature AI recruitment
- **$15,000-$30,000 saved** per position
- **66% of organizations** reduced hiring costs

### Quality
- **25-35% higher** first-year retention
- **67% improvement** in hire success rates
- **55% of companies** report more diverse hires

## Ariat GCC Recommendations

### Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Foundation
- Implement AI recruiting automation (Paradox or similar)
- Deploy AI onboarding platform
- Establish employee self-service chatbot

### Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Optimization
- Add performance management AI
- Implement workforce planning for attrition prediction
- Deploy engagement monitoring

### Phase 3 (Months 13-18): Scale
- Leverage predictive analytics for skills gaps
- Optimize based on data
- Scale without proportionally scaling HR overhead

- **$300K annual savings** (mid-market benchmark)
