## Gap Analysis: What's Missing for Colin's Slides

### Gap 1: Managed Testing + AI

**Current state:** No testing-specific AI content. "Automated Code Review & QA" is closest but focuses on CI/CD, not manual testing transformation.

**What's needed:**
- AI-assisted test case generation
- Intelligent test automation (which tests to run, prioritization)
- Visual/UI testing with AI
- Defect prediction and root cause analysis
- Test coverage analysis
- Manual testing augmentation (not replacement)

---

### Gap 2: Department-Specific AI (HR, Finance, Legal, Marketing)

**Current state:** Page 10 has KYC/Onboarding Automation (Finance-adjacent) but no HR, Legal, or Marketing content.

**What's needed:**
- **HR:** Recruiting automation, onboarding agents, policy Q&A bots, employee self-service
- **Finance:** Expense processing, report generation, anomaly detection, forecasting
- **Legal:** Contract review, compliance monitoring, document search, risk flagging
- **Marketing:** Content generation, campaign analysis, personalization, customer insights

---

### Gap 3: Culture + AI

**Current state:** Zero content on culture preservation during AI/digital transformation

**What's needed:**
- How AI enables consistent culture across geographies
- AI as knowledge preservation (institutional memory)
- AI for onboarding and training (consistent experience)
- Human-AI collaboration framing (augmentation, not replacement)
