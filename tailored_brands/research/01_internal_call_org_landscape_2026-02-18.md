## 3. The QA Political Dynamic

This is one of the most strategically significant organizational dynamics for BayOne's engagement.

### The Conflict
- Siva wants to bring QA under his organization but is "losing the battle": "Siva is trying to bring it, but he's losing the battle"
- QA currently sits under separate leadership in Houston

### Vijay Kampole — Senior Director, QA (Houston)
- **Title:** Senior Director for QA
- **Location:** Houston
- **Relationship to Siva:** There is friction. Zahra observed: "Siva's not volunteering as easily for me to set up a meeting with Vijay. Because there is some sort of... I assume there's friction"
- **Vijay's concern:** "Vijay is more worried at this point about losing his QA area to Siva, is what I'm guessing"
- **Meeting status:** BayOne was trying to get a meeting with Vijay: "we're trying to get a meeting with Vijay next. Now he says he was available this week, since he wasn't and we had a KO [kickoff]. And I didn't hear about it, I didn't push it, but it's coming, I think"

### Manoj Thora — QA (Under Vijay, Houston)
- **Reports to:** Vijay Kampole
- **Background:** Zahra worked with Manoj at Albertsons: "Manoj and I work together at Albertsons"
- **Discovery path:** Siva told Zahra that Manoj handles QA: "Siva's the one who said, oh, by the way, Manoj handles QA. And then I said, oh, I know Manoj"
- **Key misunderstanding corrected:** Zahra initially assumed QA was part of Siva's org: "I didn't know that he was QA only. I thought it was part of Siva"

### Colin's Strategic Insight on QA
Colin identified that even without formal control of QA, Siva can effectively control test generation through shift-left practices:

> "If you're writing the code, you should be testing it. But the thing is that the QA teams don't necessarily need to do anything other than verify that this just actually ran and happened. But generating the test can still happen here [in Siva's org]. So even though he doesn't have control over QA, he effectively has control over QA. Because these guys are just hitting the key and debugging and testing and recording. So that could still be part of this."

Colin further noted this is "not the dead in the water one here. Even if someone else has already started on it, maybe they haven't looked at it from that angle." This positions the shift-left / AI-assisted test generation approach as a way to work within Siva's authority without requiring Vijay's QA team to be reorganized.
