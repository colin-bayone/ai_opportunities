09

Meeting Approach

Philosophy: Listen first, don't pitch. They will share openly - let them.

Do

  • Listen and brainstorm together
  • Ask clarifying questions
  • Let them share first
  • Stroke egos carefully
  • Propose small pieces later
  • Show curiosity, not superiority

Don't

  • Pitch capabilities heavily
  • Tell them they don't know things
  • Overwhelm with case studies
  • Propose millions immediately
  • Be aggressive
  • Position as replacement

From internal team: "These guys are pretty smart. Even though they might not know a lot about AI, they'll take what you have and brainstorm with it. It's a good thing - but approach it gently."

About Priorities
