EPNM to EMS Conversion

Engagement Pricing
Project Objective

Convert roughly 250 legacy EPNM screens to the EMS platform with full functional equivalence, enabling Cisco to consolidate all customers onto a single modern architecture while preserving user experience. This engagement delivers end-to-end conversion of each screen, including the frontend UI, supporting backend logic, and EMS service integration, so that customers transitioning from EPNM experience no disruption to their existing workflows and operations.

Pricing is based on the scope and assumptions described in this document. Any material changes to scope, deliverable count, timeline, or technical requirements may be subject to a formal change request process with adjusted pricing.

Assumptions:

  • Scope: 240 to 260 screens as identified during initial codebase analysis
  • Access: Cisco-provisioned development environment and Cisco-licensed AI tooling (Claude Code or equivalent)
  • Cisco SME availability for initial context, screen prioritization, and periodic checkpoints
  • Cisco team identifies screens for conversion, with focus on functionality not yet present in EMS
  • EMS development environment available for deployment and validation
  • Access to EPNM and EMS applications available for testing when needed
  • EMS backend services and core infrastructure are substantially in place from prior migration work. Backend effort for remaining screens is expected to consist primarily of targeted integration and service adaptation rather than new service development.
Material changes to these assumptions may require a change request with adjusted pricing and timeline.
