# Role: Senior Engineer (Onshore, Bay Area)

**Location:** On-site at Cisco campus (San Jose). Physical presence is a firm requirement.

**Reports to:** Colin Moore (Director of AI)

**Relationship to Team:** Technical leader for the offshore team. Provides day-to-day guidance, sets priorities, reviews work, unblocks issues. This is a hands-on leadership role, not a management role. This person actively contributes to the technical approach and implementation while guiding others.

---

## Why This Role Exists

This engagement requires someone physically present at Cisco. They need to attend in-person meetings, build relationships with Cisco engineers, and secure access to systems and documentation. Remote-only coordination with a large enterprise like Cisco is insufficient.

This person is the bridge between BayOne's offshore delivery team and Cisco's internal teams. They are also the face of BayOne at Cisco for this project.

---

## Day-to-Day Responsibilities

**Cisco Interface:**
- Attend meetings with Cisco engineering teams (Srini, Divakar, others)
- Gather requirements, clarify scope, understand blockers
- Secure access to systems, APIs, documentation
- Build relationships with Cisco engineers
- Represent BayOne professionally

**Technical Leadership:**
- Own and guide the technical approach for the project
- Lead (not just manage) the offshore team technically
- Translate Cisco requirements into actionable work
- Conduct regular syncs with offshore engineers
- Review code and technical decisions
- Unblock technical issues by getting answers from Cisco

**Hands-On Technical Work:**
- Actively contribute to implementation, especially during discovery and early phases
- Prototype integrations, validate API access, test assumptions
- This person writes code alongside the team

**Reporting:**
- Regular updates to Colin on progress, risks, blockers
- Flag issues early before they become problems
- Provide input on technical direction and trade-offs

---

## Required Skills

**Technical:**
- Very senior Python experience (primary language for this engagement)
- Strong familiarity with databases
- Strong familiarity with AI/ML concepts and integration
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and tooling
- API integration experience (direct integration, not cloud-managed AI services)
- Comfortable navigating unfamiliar codebases and systems

**DevOps:**
- Background in DevOps practices (not necessarily a DevOps engineer, but experienced)
- Experience automating workflows or working within CI/CD systems
- Familiar with DevOps tooling beyond basic Git (Jenkins, Airflow, pipeline automation)
- Able to set direction and communicate best practices to both the team and Cisco

**AI and Tooling:**
- Proficient with AI pair programming tools (Copilot, Claude, Cursor, etc.)
- Familiarity with agentic AI concepts
- Experience with LangChain/LangGraph or similar frameworks preferred

**Communication and Presence:**
- Professional polish - represents BayOne to a major client
- Excellent people skills
- Can explain technical concepts clearly to engineers and stakeholders
- Comfortable in meetings with senior stakeholders
- Can navigate a large, complex organization

**Working Style:**
- Startup mindset: iterative, scrappy, figures things out
- Self-directed; doesn't need hand-holding
- Comfortable with ambiguity and incomplete information
- Can context-switch between leadership and hands-on work

## Preferred Experience

- 5+ years of software engineering experience
- Past work on similar engagements (CI/CD tooling, developer productivity, enterprise integrations)
- Experience working with enterprise clients or in large organizations
- Experience leading or mentoring other engineers
- Airflow experience preferred (critical integration point for this project)

- Offshore team is rarely blocked waiting for information from Cisco
