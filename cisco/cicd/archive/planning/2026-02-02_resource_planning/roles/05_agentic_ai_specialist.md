# Role: Agentic AI Specialist (Offshore)

**Location:** Offshore (India)

**Reports to:** Senior Engineer (Onshore), with oversight from Colin Moore (Director of AI)

**Relationship to Team:** Senior AI role, above the AI Engineer in the AI hierarchy. Acts as a mentor and technical lead for the AI Engineer on agentic and autonomous system work. Owns the most complex AI work in the engagement - building systems that take real actions autonomously.

---

## Why This Role Exists

The Self-Healing capability is the most complex part of this engagement. It requires building AI systems that don't just respond to queries or analyze data - they take autonomous actions in production environments. This requires specialized expertise in agentic AI patterns, decision-making logic, governance frameworks, and safety mechanisms.

This is a distinct skillset from general AI/NLP work. The AI Engineer handles chat, diagnosis, and text-based AI features. The Agentic AI Specialist handles systems that act.

---

## Day-to-Day Responsibilities

**Agentic System Development:**
- Design and build autonomous decision-making systems
- Implement governance frameworks (what can be auto-corrected, what requires human approval)
- Build safety mechanisms and rollback capabilities
- Create action triggers that integrate with orchestration tools (Airflow, Jenkins)

**Technical Leadership (AI):**
- Mentor and guide the AI Engineer
- Provide technical direction on AI architecture decisions
- Review AI-related code and approaches
- Help the AI Engineer grow in agentic concepts

**Collaboration:**
- Work closely with the backend engineer on integration points for automated actions
- Coordinate with onshore lead on governance discussions with the client
- Participate in team syncs

**Foundation Work (Early Involvement):**
- Even before Self-Healing deliverables, involved in understanding the systems
- Helps shape the AI architecture from early phases
- Supports general AI work alongside the AI Engineer

---

## Required Skills

**Agentic AI Expertise (Expert Level):**
- Deep experience building agentic AI systems (systems that take actions, not just respond)
- Understanding of autonomous decision-making patterns
- Strong understanding of system design patterns and governance for AI models, especially those acting autonomously on production/enterprise systems
- Experience with governance and safety for AI systems
- Familiarity with agent frameworks and patterns (e.g., LangChain, LangGraph, Vanna, or similar - specific frameworks TBD based on client requirements)

**System-Level Thinking:**
- Can design systems that safely take real actions in production
- Understands failure modes and how to build resilient autonomous systems
- Thinks through edge cases and safety implications

**Python:**
- Extremely strong Python developer
- Can work at the system level, not just application level

**Deployment:**
- Experience deploying AI systems on-prem
- Understands infrastructure requirements for agentic workloads

**AI Tooling:**
- Proficient with AI pair programming tools (Claude Code, Cursor, or similar)
- Deep familiarity with agentic AI development patterns

---

## Experience Level

**Seniority:** Senior-to-Expert (5-8+ years), specialized in agentic AI

- Proven experience building autonomous/agentic AI systems
- Prior work on systems that take real actions (not just chatbots or analysis)
- Experience defining governance frameworks for AI
- Ideally has worked on CI/CD, DevOps, or infrastructure automation with AI

---

## What Success Looks Like

- Self-Healing system works reliably and safely
- Governance framework is clear and well-implemented
- AI Engineer grows under their mentorship
- Autonomous actions are taken correctly with appropriate safeguards
- No incidents from automated actions going wrong
