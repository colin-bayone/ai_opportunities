# AI Engineer

**BayOne Solutions | Remote (Offshore)**

## Position Overview

BayOne Solutions is hiring an AI Engineer to work on a high-visibility engagement with a Fortune 500 company. You will be embedded with an elite, fast-paced team that leverages AI-assisted development as a core part of their workflow. This role is for someone who lives and breathes AI tooling—staying current on the latest releases, understanding when AI is the right solution versus when deterministic approaches make more sense, and helping the team continuously improve how they work.

This is a hands-on engineering role. You will build AI-powered automation, develop Claude Code skills and agents, and collaborate closely with engineering, QA, and QE teams to explore and introduce new AI tools and workflows. You should be proactive, take ownership of problems, and engage deeply with the team rather than waiting for direction.

## Key Responsibilities

### AI-Assisted Development & Tooling (40%)

- Work daily with AI pair programming tools (Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub Copilot) as a core part of your development workflow
- Stay current on the rapidly evolving AI tooling landscape and evaluate new tools as they emerge
- Develop judgment for when AI-assisted approaches are appropriate versus when deterministic solutions are better
- Help the team adopt and refine AI-powered workflows that improve velocity and quality
- Identify opportunities to automate repetitive tasks through AI tooling
- Share knowledge and best practices with the broader team

### Agent & Skill Development (40%)

- Build Claude Code skills, agents, and sub-agents that automate development workflows
- Develop production-grade automation using Python and agent frameworks (Claude Agent SDK, LangChain, LangGraph, or similar)
- Create tools that integrate with team systems (GitHub, issue tracking, internal APIs)
- Implement robust error handling, logging, and fallback mechanisms for AI-powered automation
- Iterate quickly based on team feedback and real-world usage

### Team Collaboration & Process (20%)

- Collaborate closely with engineering, QA, and QE teams on shared workflows and tooling
- Participate actively in team discussions, bringing ideas and solutions rather than waiting for direction
- Communicate progress, blockers, and technical decisions clearly
- Document tools, workflows, and patterns for team adoption
- Help onboard team members to AI-assisted development practices

## Required Qualifications

### AI Tools and Judgment

- 2+ years of hands-on experience working with AI/ML systems or AI-assisted development tools
- Deep proficiency with AI pair programming tools (Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, or similar)
- Strong understanding of current AI tooling landscape and ability to evaluate emerging tools
- Good judgment on when to use AI-assisted approaches versus deterministic solutions
- Experience building agents, skills, or automation using LLM-based tools
- Understanding of prompt engineering and how to get reliable results from AI systems

### Software Engineering

- 2+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong Python proficiency as your primary language
- Experience building automation, scripts, and developer tooling
- Solid understanding of Git workflows, APIs, and system integration
- Comfort working with ambiguity and iterating quickly

### Working Style and Communication

- Proactive problem solver who takes ownership and drives work forward independently
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment with high expectations
- Collaborative mindset—works well with engineering, QA, and QE teams
- Self-directed and engaged; doesn't wait for detailed instructions

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in enterprise retail, e-commerce, or consumer-facing technology environments
- Background working on high-performance teams with tight iteration cycles
- Experience building Claude Code skills, custom agents, or workflow automation
- Familiarity with the Claude Agent SDK or similar agent development frameworks
- Prior work in consulting or client-facing delivery environments
- Experience introducing and championing new tools within a team
