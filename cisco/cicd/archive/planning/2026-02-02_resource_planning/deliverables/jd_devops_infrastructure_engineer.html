DevOps / Infrastructure Engineer

BayOne Solutions | Remote (Offshore)

Position Overview

BayOne Solutions is hiring a DevOps / Infrastructure Engineer to manage deployment infrastructure and CI/CD operations for a major enterprise client. You will own the on-prem deployment environment, support AI and backend service deployments, and ensure reliable operation of production systems.

Key Responsibilities

Infrastructure & Deployment (60%)

  • Manage on-prem deployment infrastructure for backend and AI services
  • Configure and maintain CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins and Apache Airflow
  • Set up and manage containerized environments using Docker
  • Ensure reliable, repeatable deployments across development and production environments
  • Troubleshoot infrastructure issues and optimize system performance

Monitoring & Operations (25%)

  • Implement monitoring and alerting using Grafana and related tools
  • Maintain observability across services and infrastructure
  • Respond to production incidents and coordinate resolution
  • Document infrastructure architecture and operational procedures

Collaboration (15%)

  • Support AI and backend engineers with deployment requirements
  • Coordinate with the on-site technical lead on infrastructure decisions
  • Participate in team syncs and communicate progress
  • Proactively identify infrastructure risks and propose solutions

Required Qualifications

  • 3-5+ years of experience in DevOps, infrastructure, or site reliability engineering
  • Strong experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation
  • Experience with Jenkins and workflow orchestration tools
  • Proficiency with Docker and containerized deployments
  • Experience managing on-prem infrastructure
  • Strong Linux administration skills
  • Scripting proficiency in Python or Bash
  • Experience with monitoring and observability tools
  • Proactive communicator across time zones
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with Apache Airflow administration
  • Background in supporting AI/ML service deployments
  • Experience with Grafana for monitoring and dashboards
  • Familiarity with enterprise security and compliance requirements
  • Proficiency with AI-assisted development tools such as Claude Code
  • Experience working with distributed teams

Application Process
