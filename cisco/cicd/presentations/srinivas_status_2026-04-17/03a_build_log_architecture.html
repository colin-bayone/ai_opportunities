Proposed End-to-End Architecture

Initial thinking grounded in the infrastructure review and the pain point data. Apache Airflow wraps the full pipeline for orchestration, retries, dead-letter behavior, and observability.

Simplest first, always. Deterministic regex precedes ML, which precedes LLM. Each error is caught at exactly one tier. The bulk falls out cheaply; only novel or compound errors reach the language model, where root cause reasoning and fix suggestion carry their cost.
