Pain Point Analysis: Engagement Patterns
Engagement Patterns

What the Channel Really Carries

Two supporting views of the channel. Originals and replies by category show where engagement concentrates. Non-resolvable response times show where the channel is carrying non-technical traffic that never receives a technical resolution path.

Originals Versus Replies by Category
How engagement concentrates across categories
Status Update and Off-topic threads attract the highest reply-to-original ratios, while Test Failure and Code Review see many originals with relatively few replies. Technical threads trigger less conversational engagement than announcements and social chatter.
Response Time — Non-Resolvable Categories
Categories where a technical reply is not the expected path
Tutorial, Off-topic, Uncategorized, and Task Assignment threads show response times in the hundreds to thousands of minutes with very low coverage (4 to 33 percent). Much of this traffic is not pursued as an actionable technical thread.

A significant portion of channel volume is not technical resolution traffic. Subdividing off-topic into genuine social chatter, image and link shares, and uncategorized items separates the signal from the noise. An AI triage layer needs to route each stream differently.
