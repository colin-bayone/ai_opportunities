## Key Findings

1. **Saurav is the stronger starter.** Has both laptops, has prior briefing context from Colin, asks the most and best questions (architectural, logistical, governance-related). He will be productive earliest.

2. **Askari is behind but capable.** Still at Ultratech Cement (in crisis — coal and petcoke supply chain disruption), BayOne laptop only shipped March 18, first exposure to all engagement details. Background as lead AI engineer and data science master positions him well for the AI-heavy work. He will need more ramp time.

3. **Colin's management style is deliberately different from BayOne norms.** He frames the team as BayOne-first, not client-first. He gives ground rules as a numbered list (hardware, peer interaction, dual-platform comms, early escalation). He is strategically transparent — sharing his playbook for winning Srinivas's loyalty through DeepSight — while carefully filtering out BayOne internal dysfunction.

4. **Colin omits all negative history.** No mention of Sarang's failure, BayOne organizational problems, pricing negotiations, or internal politics. The team receives a clean, optimistic, well-structured briefing. This is a deliberate onboarding choice.

5. **The three-tier self-healing framework is new.** Colin introduces Type 1 (RCA/informational), Type 2 (deterministic button-click actions), and Type 3 (AI-driven self-healing) as distinct categories. This framework was not explicit in prior sets. His insistence on governance for Type 3 is the strongest statement in the library about protecting BayOne from liability.

6. **Two team members are still missing.** Srikar expected next week (week of March 24). Namita delayed by H1B transfer — Colin is skeptical of the "next week" promises and expects her before end of March at best.

7. **Colin positions Zahra and Neha as intelligence assets, R2 as the escalation path.** The team receives no indication of the Zahra/Colin direct channel or the internal BayOne dynamics. R2 (Rahul Bobbili) is presented as the backup to Colin for unblocking.

8. **The formal plan of attack was not covered.** Colin ran out of time. The team was asked to review documents independently. The next meeting (rescheduled to March 19 to accommodate Eid) will cover the solution plan.
