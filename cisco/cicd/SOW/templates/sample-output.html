2 Services and Work Product

2.1 Services Objectives

Organization background:

This scope is being requested by Cisco's Cloud Networking Group (CN) Test team. This team is responsible for validating (regression and automation tests) the generic engineering releases and customer specials (specific customer requests) releases for Nexus 9000 (switches) product line along with the development of required infrastructure tools. Cisco NX-OS (Nexus operating system) Software is a data center-class operating system built with modularity, resiliency, and serviceability at its foundation. Cisco NX-OS helps ensure continuous availability for mission-critical data center environments and provides a robust feature set that fulfils the switching and storage networking needs of data centers.

Outsourced:

To determine the best user experience in upcoming NX-OS releases, Cisco needs to execute automation and regression testing of the features in an upcoming NX-OS release.

Impact/Benefit:

Apart from cost saving, the other benefits of this service is speed of delivery, quality and consistency with Cisco internal brand guidelines.

Supplier management of resource:

The supplier will manage the Service outcome along with the resources. The supplier will be responsible for managing the resources in regard to ensuring they understand and complete the project to satisfaction as well as provide any necessary training. The supplier is responsible for replacing the resource with minimal to no disruption to Cisco or the Service. Supplier is responsible for the service outcome. Supplier has a lead who is primary point of contact and all work is routed through the lead.
