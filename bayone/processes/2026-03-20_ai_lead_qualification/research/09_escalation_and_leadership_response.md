# Escalation Timeline and Leadership Response

## How This Reached Leadership

### The Pattern (Individual Level)
Colin has been pushing back on each incident individually through direct messages to salespeople. This has been working tactically (Anuj course-corrected on HPE, Rima's boss corrected on Tailored Brands presentation approach), but the systemic problem persists.

### The Escalation (Mar 1)
Colin forwarded the HPE/Pratik email chain to Yogesh and Rahul with a note establishing the pattern: "I've been running into a recurring challenge with the sales team where opportunities are being brought to me before any real qualification has been done."

### Rahul's Email to Leadership (Mar 4)
Rahul (President) sent an email to Surej (CEO), Colin, Anuj, Amit, Richa, cc'ing Yogesh:

Key points from Rahul:
- "Colin is getting approached by multiple salespeople with similar questions"
- "The team is involving him very early in the sales process, which is starting to distract him from his day-to-day priorities"
- Proposed a knowledge workshop and supporting content
- "Put the right process in place so we can respond thoughtfully and accurately"
- **"Clearly determine which opportunities we should pursue and which we should not"**

### Anuj's Response (Mar 4)
Proposed two concrete actions:
1. A specific AI capabilities PPT for the sales team with case studies and value areas
2. A template of specific questions that Colin wants the team to ask during initial discovery

Wanted this done "ideally this week."

### Surej's Response (Mar 4)
CEO agreed: "Let me work with Colin and Priya to address this. Want to ensure that we do this without losing any potential opportunities."

### Richa's Input (Mar 4)
Suggested separate case studies by AI area (Software Dev, Data/Analytics, Automation) to help sales position the right capability early.

### Priya's Independent Validation (Mar 9)
CTO reached the same conclusions independently when reviewing Tailored Brands materials:
- "It's everything on earth and hard to fine grain"
- "We should not just randomly submit to solve world hunger in a deck"
- "We might lose face"
- "We need to have a clear plan of what we are trying to address"

---

## What Leadership Agreed On

1. **Workshop for the sales team** to build AI literacy
2. **Capabilities deck** that sales can use without needing Colin in the room
3. **Discovery question template** so sales knows what to ask
4. **Process for when Colin gets involved** (after qualification)

---

## The Opening for the Framework

Leadership has already agreed that a framework is needed. The door is open. The framework Colin builds should:
- Codify what Rahul already described (process for qualification)
- Deliver on what Anuj asked for (discovery question template)
- Address the deeper problems leadership hasn't seen yet (case study integrity, Amit's promises, capacity)
- Be practical enough for the sales team to actually follow
