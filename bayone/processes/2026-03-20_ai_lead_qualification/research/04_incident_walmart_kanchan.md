# Incident: Walmart / Kanchan (Feb 25, 2026)

## Patterns Present
- Pattern 3 (Promising things that don't exist)
- Pattern 5 (Committing Colin's time without asking)

## What Happened
Kanchan (sales) reached out asking Colin to connect about a Walmart lead. The client had questions about:
- AI accelerators BayOne has developed, including chatbot and agent-based solutions
- A demo of these capabilities
- Whether this could be a standalone product
- A feature and functionality list

None of these things exist at BayOne.

## Root Cause: Amit (VP of Delivery)
- Amit had previously brought this up to Colin. Colin told him directly that BayOne doesn't have pre-built AI accelerators, chatbot demos, or agent-based products
- Amit went ahead and sent a deck to Walmart anyway, without looping Colin in or showing him what was sent
- The client now has expectations based on capabilities that were fabricated
- Kanchan is innocent in this. She's relaying what her boss told her to relay

## Key Dynamic
Amit is volunteering other people's labor to impress clients. He's faking it, but Colin's team would be the ones who'd have to make it. Colin specifically noted: "He confuses case studies from real work I did at my prior org with demo-able work that BayOne can realistically claim."

## Colin's Response (Final)
Warm to Kanchan, pointed at Amit. Key elements:
- Made it clear Amit had been told once already
- Noted he never saw the deck sent to Walmart
- Refused to own commitments made without his input
- Redirected Kanchan toward what BayOne actually offers
- Offered to talk to Amit directly so Kanchan doesn't have to carry the bad news to her own boss

## Outcome
Paper trail established showing Colin flagged this issue and wasn't consulted on the Walmart deck.

## New Insight for Framework
**Delivery leadership is making sales promises.** This isn't just a sales team problem. Amit (Delivery) is promising capabilities that don't exist and then expecting Colin's team to produce them. The framework needs to cover who is authorized to make commitments to clients about AI capabilities.
