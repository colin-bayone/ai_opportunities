# Session Goals

## Primary Objective
Build a lead qualification framework for BayOne Solutions' sales team covering all AI initiatives.

## Why This Matters
The sales team repeatedly brings unqualified, vague, or fabricated opportunities to Colin (Director of AI), wasting his time and creating client expectations that can't be met. The framework needs to address the root causes, not just add a checklist.

## Approach
1. Document existing problem patterns from the sales-response-buddy skill (source material)
2. Extract additional patterns from transcript sessions Colin will provide
3. Identify the big problem statements that the framework must solve
4. Design a lead qualification framework that directly addresses each problem
5. Make it usable by the sales team without requiring AI expertise

## Deliverable
A practical lead qualification framework that the sales team can follow before involving Colin or any AI technical resource.
