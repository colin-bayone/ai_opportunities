Technical Manager, AI Engineering

BayOne Solutions | India (Remote)

Position Overview

BayOne Solutions is hiring a Technical Manager, AI Engineering to lead and grow our internal AI engineering team. Reporting directly to the Director of AI, you will serve as the primary technical and leadership resource for a team of 5-15 direct reports, handling day-to-day management while remaining deeply involved in technical direction.

This is a hands-on leadership role. You will manage projects, conduct interviews, grow the team, and ensure your engineers have clear direction and adequate resources. You will also stay close to the work itself, reviewing architecture decisions, unblocking technical problems, and maintaining the engineering standards that make the team effective.

We are looking for someone who has led AI teams before, not someone transitioning into leadership. Your most recent role should demonstrate experience managing AI engineers, with strong technical depth across multiple AI domains including agentic systems, machine learning, or production AI infrastructure.

The right candidate wants to build something: impactful production-ready solutions, an incredible team that you manage and mentor, and a growing AI solutions portfolio. You should operate with autonomy on day-to-day decisions while knowing when to escalate, and bring the technical depth to guide your team's work firsthand.

Key Responsibilities

Team Leadership (40%)

  • Provide day-to-day direction and support to your engineering team
  • Keep projects organized, resourced, and moving forward
  • Remove blockers and resolve issues before they require escalation
  • Run standups, planning sessions, and regular team syncs
  • Track project status, timelines, and deliverables
  • Report progress, risks, and resource needs to the Director of AI
  • Handle administrative responsibilities so engineers can focus on engineering

Technical Direction (25%)

  • Guide the team's technical approach, coordinating with the Director of AI on significant architectural decisions
  • Maintain quality standards across the team's output
  • Participate in design discussions and weigh in on technical trade-offs
  • Ensure consistency in tooling, frameworks, and engineering practices
  • Keep current with developments in AI/ML and bring relevant ideas back to the team
  • Know when a decision is routine versus when it warrants broader input

Hands-on Development (20%)

  • Contribute directly to engineering work when the team needs support
  • Own accountability for deliverables alongside your team, not above them
  • Work through complex technical problems with engineers rather than delegating from a distance
  • Conduct code reviews and provide substantive technical feedback
  • Prototype solutions or validate approaches when it accelerates the team

Team Growth (15%)

  • Conduct interviews and make hiring recommendations to expand the team
  • Onboard new engineers and set them up for success
  • Mentor engineers on both technical skills and professional development
  • Identify skill gaps and create opportunities to address them
  • Build a team culture that attracts and retains strong talent

Required Qualifications

The ideal candidate brings extensive experience, generally around eight years across AI, data engineering, ML engineering, or related technical fields.

Leadership Experience

  • 3+ years of experience leading an AI engineering team
  • Demonstrated ability to manage, mentor, and grow engineers
  • Track record of delivering AI projects through a team you led
  • Experience conducting technical interviews and making hiring decisions
  • Experience coordinating and managing teams of 5 or more engineers

Technical Breadth

Demonstrated expertise across the following areas, with depth in multiple disciplines preferred:

  • Agentic AI and large language models
  • Machine learning, statistical modeling, and data engineering
  • Computer vision and manufacturing applications
  • AI infrastructure and production deployment
  • Modern AI tooling and frameworks

Technical Requirements

  • Deep proficiency in Python
  • Strong background in Azure cloud services
  • Proficiency with AI orchestration frameworks such as LangGraph, LangChain, or equivalent
  • Fluency in GitHub workflows and DevOps practices
  • Ability to review code, guide architecture, and contribute directly when needed

Working Style

  • Detail-oriented and task-focused
  • Operates independently while maintaining alignment with leadership
  • Makes decisions confidently but knows when to seek guidance
  • Comfortable balancing management responsibilities with technical contribution
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to engage senior stakeholders both internal and external to BayOne

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience leading a team through full product lifecycle: build, deploy, and production management
  • Experience scaling a team through a growth phase
  • Background in enterprise environments with security and compliance considerations
  • Familiarity with Apache Airflow, PostgreSQL, or similar production data infrastructure
  • Familiarity with on-prem deployments alongside cloud infrastructure
  • Exposure to budget planning and resource allocation
  • Experience managing multiple concurrent projects or workstreams
  • Proficiency with AI-assisted development tools such as Claude Code, Cursor, or GitHub Copilot
