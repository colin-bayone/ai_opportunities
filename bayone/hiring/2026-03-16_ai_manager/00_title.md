# Decision: Position Title

**Decided:** Technical Manager, AI Engineering

**Alternatives Considered:**
- AI Engineering Manager
- Manager, AI Engineering
- Lead AI Engineer
- AI Technical Manager
- AI Team Lead

**Rationale:** Emphasizes both the technical depth and management responsibility. "Technical Manager" signals this isn't a pure people-manager role; the person needs hands-on capability. "AI Engineering" scopes the domain clearly.
