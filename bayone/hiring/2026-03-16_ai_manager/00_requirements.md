# AI Manager Position - Requirements

## Position Context

- **Company:** BayOne Solutions (Internal hire, not client placement)
- **Reports To:** Director of AI (Colin Moore)
- **Team Size:** 5-15 engineers
- **Location:** India (Remote)
- **Role Type:** Technical Manager, AI Engineering

## Hard Requirements (Non-Negotiable)

### Experience
- **3+ years leading an AI team** - not another discipline, specifically AI
- **Must be their most recent role** - no career-changers or people returning to leadership
- **Primary language: Python** - no exceptions
- **Azure background required**
- **LangGraph experience required** (and similar frameworks)

### Technical Breadth
Must be highly qualified across **at least 2** of these 5 areas:
1. Agentic AI and language models
2. Machine learning, statistical, and data engineering
3. Computer vision and manufacturing
4. Infrastructure and production deployments
5. Using a modern tool stack for any of these

### Leadership Profile
- Strong engineering instinct AND leadership maturity
- Technical lead AND manager (dual role)
- Detail-oriented, task-focused, independent
- Makes decisions autonomously but knows when to escalate
- Not afraid to ask for guidance
- Won't go rogue or pursue wrong directions

## Role Purpose

**Primary goal: Act as Director's proxy and make Director's life easier.**

Key functions:
- Day-to-day management of engineering team
- Keep team organized and managed
- Resolve small problems independently
- Know when to escalate to Director
- Take interviews, grow the team
- Remain highly aligned with Director (technical + team capacity)
- Manage, track, and administrate projects
- Ensure team resources are adequately provisioned

## Culture Fit

- Wants to build and grow a team under them
- Gives proper direction (not hands-off)
- Gets in the trenches with the team
- Likes to dig in and get involved

## Anti-Patterns (What We Don't Want)

- Super senior (30+ year veterans) unless recent experience is extremely relevant
- Anyone whose AI leadership experience isn't recent
- Hands-off managers who just delegate
- People without actual AI team leadership (not adjacent fields)

## Notes on Seniority

- Recent experience is highest priority
- Looking for emerging leaders, not retiring executives
- 3 years minimum, but right fit matters more than maximum years

1. **Job Description (candidate-facing)**
   - Professional, clear
   - Attracts right candidates while filtering wrong ones

2. **Recruiter Guide (internal)**
   - Screening criteria
   - How to evaluate abstract qualities
   - Clear metrics and firm requirements
   - Interview guidance
