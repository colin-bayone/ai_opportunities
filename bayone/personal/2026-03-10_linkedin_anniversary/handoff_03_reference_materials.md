## Document 4: TalentAI - Award Submission

### What TalentAI Is
TalentAI is an AI-first enterprise recruitment intelligence platform built internally at BayOne. It is an INTERNAL tool, NOT a public product. Colin said "we'll be exploring different options for it in the coming months ahead" and "who knows what the future has in store for that." It should be referenced as an internal platform, not positioned as a product for sale.

### Key TalentAI Capabilities
- Universal semantic search combining keyword, fuzzy, and semantic matching
- AI-powered resume parsing built from scratch (not third-party) for enterprise-grade accuracy
- Intelligent candidate-job matching with transparent, traceable scoring
- Resume authenticity validation catching AI-generated and keyword-stuffed profiles
- Candidate feedback and alignment visibility
- Pre-qualified talent pool creating a flywheel of engaged candidates
- Responsible AI with full auditability, no black-box algorithms
- Built-in AI chat assistant and agentic workflow engine
- Code assessments for technical roles (baseline and AI-assisted development)
- MCQ assessments for domain knowledge
- Integrated email and communications
- AI-summarized meeting transcripts
- SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliant, deployable on Azure, GCP, or AWS

### AI Technologies Used
- Language Models for parsing, extraction, chat, summarization
- Vision Models for document image extraction
- Embedding Models for semantic search and similarity scoring
- Multi-Algorithm Search combining keyword, fuzzy, and semantic approaches
- AI Providers: Azure OpenAI and Anthropic with multi-provider orchestration

### TalentAI Impact Metrics (from award submission)
- Resume analysis reduced from 15-60 minutes to under 2 minutes per candidate
- 50-70% reduction in time to identify qualified candidates
- 20-40% more qualified candidates discovered vs. keyword search
- Search precision improved from 30-40% to 75-90%
- 60-80% reduction in false positive candidates
- 25-40% improvement in interview-to-offer conversion rates
- 80-95% candidate-job alignment accuracy
- 4-6 separate tools consolidated into one platform
- 30-50% of rejected candidates return for future opportunities

### Award Won
ELETS India AI Impact Summit Award for AI in Recruitment, Screening and Evaluation. This is the category: "AI Enterprise & Application Award - AI in Recruitment, Screening & Evaluation."

### Team Composition
The TalentAI development team is roughly 80% female. This emerged organically through merit-based hiring. It reflects BayOne's #MakeTechPurple initiative.
