# Capabilities Deck Structure Brainstorm

## Document Purpose

A polished, VP-ready capabilities deck for BayOne Solutions to showcase:
- Who BayOne is
- AI capabilities and solution areas
- Key technologies and approaches
- Operating model
- Case studies / engagement highlights

**Audience:** VP-level tech buyers (immediate: Cisco VP, but deck should work broadly)
**Tone:** Big Four consulting. Confident, substantive, metric-driven. Not vanilla. No fluff.

---

## Proposed Structure

### 1. Cover
- BayOne Solutions
- "AI Solutions Partner"
- Clean, branded

### 2. Who We Are
**Purpose:** Establish credibility quickly

Content options:
- Talent + Solutions company (not just staffing)
- Colin Moore, Director of AI (technical depth)
- Hybrid model: Onshore leadership + offshore scale
- Existing client relationships (major logos without overstating)

**Stat cards (need to be defensible):**
- Years in business / client count (need from Rahul)
- Industries served
- Client logos tier (Cisco, Salesforce, Intel, etc.)

### 3. The AI Execution Gap (Market Framing)
**Purpose:** Why this matters now. Set up the "so what"

Possible angles:
- Enterprises know AI is important but struggle to execute
- POCs that never reach production
- Data chaos blocking AI initiatives
- Skills gap (Mirel's team is a perfect example)
- Only 12% of firms can execute multi-modal AI well

**Do NOT:** Generic "AI is changing everything" slides. Be specific.

### 4. Our Approach
**Purpose:** How BayOne is different

Key messages:
- 70-80% reusable architecture, 20-30% customization
- 4-12 week POCs (fast to value)
- POC → Production pathway
- Build on your stack (not external products)
- Internal-first: we use what we sell

Possible visual: Discovery → POC → Production pipeline

### 5. Solution Architecture Overview (Five Pillars)
**Purpose:** Show breadth without overwhelming

Five pillars with one-line descriptions:
1. Developer Productivity Suite
2. Enterprise Automation & Services
3. Data & Analytics Intelligence
4. Document Intelligence Platform
5. Manufacturing & Operations Intelligence (or "Applied AI & Intelligent Systems")

**Design:** Cards or icons, high-level. Detail comes in subsequent slides.

### 13. Engagement Model
**Purpose:** How we work

Three phases:
1. **Discovery** (1-2 weeks): Understand your stack, identify quick wins
2. **POC** (4-8 weeks): Prove value with working solution
3. **Production** (8-12 weeks): Scale, harden, integrate

Model options:
- T&M (flexible, discovery)
- Fixed-price by milestone
- Managed service (ongoing)

Staffing:
- Onshore leadership + offshore scale
- Flexible pods (AI, Data, Full-stack, UI)

### 14. Why BayOne
**Purpose:** Differentiators

- **On-Premise Capable:** IP-sensitive, air-gapped, regulated environments
