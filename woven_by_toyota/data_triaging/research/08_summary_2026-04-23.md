Travis's technical presentation showed surface-layer AI fluency with architect-level framing but limited engineering depth; his claim that ground truth rules change daily does not hold up under scrutiny and is more likely a symptom of overfitting, inter-rater drift, taxonomy churn, or overly granular category decomposition.
