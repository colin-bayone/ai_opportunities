## Executed Deep-Dive Files

### 08a_meeting_problem_statement_2026-04-23.md
**Rationale:** Captures the work Travis actually described (dynamic root cause analysis, 150 to 440 categories, 40 percent AutoTriage accuracy) and the technical critique of the architectural claim that ground truth rules shift daily. The problem statement is the single most important thing to get right for any downstream decision.

### 08b_meeting_the_ask_2026-04-23.md
**Rationale:** Captures the specific commercial ask as defined on the call. One backfill role as the immediate ask, 13x scale target as the deferred ask, sourcing constraints (Palo Alto or Ann Arbor, Japanese fluency, no sponsorship, rate ceiling mismatched to preferred qualifications). Separated from positioning because the ask is about what Woven wants; positioning is about what BayOne said about itself.

### 08c_meeting_bayone_positioning_2026-04-23.md
**Rationale:** Captures how Pratik, the BayOne technical lead, and Jesse each positioned BayOne on the call, and the outcome of that framing. Pratik's "we do not have a product for this particular one" statement is the pivotal framing and requires careful interpretation. The statement reflects BayOne's Hard Rule 9 posture (solution, never product; custom tailor-made solutions using proven methodology) and differentiates BayOne from the 20-plus product vendors Travis has been evaluating. It is not a statement that closes the solutions door; it is a statement that BayOne does not compete on SKU shelf.

### 08d_meeting_travis_technical_presentation_2026-04-23.md
**Rationale:** INTERNAL ONLY. Captures what Travis said technically on the call and an assessment of his technical depth relative to the architect-level role he claims. The assessment is relevant because it determines whether a solutions conversation with this stakeholder is viable. The file does not surface externally.

### 08e_source_hireart_jd_analysis_2026-04-23.md
**Rationale:** Analyzes the job description shared by Travis during the call. The JD names the incumbent staffing vendor (HireArt) via the platform and benefits language, confirms the work shape as root cause analysis explicitly, and reveals the structural gap between the preferred qualifications (AI-literate engineer) and the pay rate (standard QA contractor rate). Held internally only given the inadvertent nature of the incumbent disclosure.
