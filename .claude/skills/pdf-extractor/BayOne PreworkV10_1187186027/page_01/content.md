**Pre-Work**

We look forward to meeting you at the BayOne Elevate_Sales Transformation Workshop. To maximize the workshop’s impact, please share your perspective on the current business environment and the opportunities you are pursuing. Your insights will help shape our shared path to excellence.

Collecting your thoughts and responding to the questions is likely to take about 30 minutes of your time at best. Your responses will remain confidential. Each question requires only ‘crisp bullet-point’ answers in a few words that capture your key thoughts.

Please send us your completed pre-work via email to anand@salesoxigen.com by 20th March, it will help us in our preparations for the workshop.

Thanks in advance, for your contribution!

**Instructions**

1.  Read “Categories of Change for Your Customers,” and provide few examples of changes that you can identify for your customers.
2.  Review the definition of a Complex Sale and select qualified opportunities to work with during the selling process. The “Data Gathering” worksheet will allow you to briefly gather data for three opportunities you are currently working on. The best opportunities will be those which are not at a too early stage.
3.  Print the worksheet pages and bring them to the session, where you will discuss and create strategies for these opportunities.
