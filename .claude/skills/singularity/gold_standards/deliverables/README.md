## How to Use

When producing a new deliverable, find the gold standard closest to your deliverable type and read it to understand content structure, section flow, and level of detail. The gold standard shows both the visual design (BayOne CSS) AND the content architecture.

Do not use these as fill-in templates. They contain real engagement content. Use them as structural references — replicate the section structure and adapt the content for the new engagement.
