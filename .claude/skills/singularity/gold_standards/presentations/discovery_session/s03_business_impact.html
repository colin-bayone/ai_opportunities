01 The Problem

Business Impact

Throttled Productivity

Support efficiency is constrained by the inability to share knowledge safely across customer boundaries. Engineers spend time re-solving problems that have already been resolved in a different customer context.

Blocked Feedback Loop

The troubleshooting workflow is designed to drive resolution at the lowest tier. The feedback loop from escalation back to self-help is broken because cleaned solutions cannot re-enter the general knowledge base.

Repeated Escalation Costs

Problems that have already been solved get re-escalated because the solutions are locked behind customer restrictions. This costs time, money, and customer trust.

Fragmented Search

Over-restriction has driven the creation of multiple segmented search systems, each covering a different knowledge pool with different access rules, compounding the problem rather than solving it.
