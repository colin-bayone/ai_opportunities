05 BayOne's Approach

Common Data Plane and Azure

Both detection layers are unified through a common data plane: a single, governed layer with centralized ingestion, IAM and RBAC integration, and compliance controls that IT teams can manage directly.

Why Azure
Azure Advantage Description
Scalability Auto-scales with usage. The system grows with the organization without requiring infrastructure re-architecture.
Maintainability Accessible to IT teams to manage, monitor, and operate directly. Detection rules, entity dictionaries, and classification models are updated through standard tooling.
Compliance integration Native integration with identity and access management, role-based access control, and data governance capabilities out of the box.
Cost efficiency Enterprise Azure services like Purview and AI Foundry provide mature, supported capabilities at a fraction of the cost of building and maintaining equivalent custom infrastructure.
Production maturity Custom or on-premises deployments can succeed, but the maturity tends to remain at proof-of-concept level rather than scaling into operational systems.

What this means for the team: This is a custom solution built on Lam's own environment using proven methodology. BayOne builds it, and the team can maintain and extend it. The goal is to empower the team to own and operate the capability independently.

A note on infrastructure: Understanding the relationship between the initiative team and the corporate Azure environment is important for scoping the deployment model accurately. The earlier this is understood, the more accurately we can scope the work.
