04 Proof of Concept Target

Selecting the Proof of Concept Target

To scope a meaningful proof of concept, we need to anchor on one specific application.

Starting point: If the prior work was tested against a specific application, that application may still be the right anchor. Alternatively, the team may have identified a different, more impactful target since the prior effort. Either path can serve as the starting point.

Signals That Can Help Identify the Right Target
Which application is the most critical to daily operations?
Which one has the most active users?
Which one is the largest and most mature?

If BayOne is given visibility into the application landscape through a catalog or further conversations with the engineering team, we can help identify the highest-impact starting point.
