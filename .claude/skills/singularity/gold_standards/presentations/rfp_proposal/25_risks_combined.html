Proactive risk identification and mitigation strategies for a successful transition

Risk Mitigation
KT & Documentation Availability
  • McGrath facilitation of KT plan to help BayOne ramp efficiently and minimize transition delays
Infrastructure Support
  • Infrastructure teams available to provide environment, system accesses, and approvals
Information Unavailability
  • All required information shared by incumbent in a timely manner; delays may impact key SLAs
Delayed Sign-offs & Approvals
  • Sign off Entry / Exit criteria
  • All necessary approvals, software, and timely accesses
Uncertainty (ticket volume, integration stability, data quality, system complexity)
  • Discovery phase (Phase 1) with robust knowledge acquisition, shadow and reverse shadow phases
  • Leveraging relevant tools and automation
Changing Geopolitical Scenarios
  • BayOne operates across USA, Canada, Philippines, Costa Rica, and India with growing global presence
Information Sharing by Incumbent
  • BayOne team already embedded in McGrath's ecosystem
  • Rebadging critical resources with McGrath's approval
  • Early on-boarding and tools-driven knowledge transition
Knowledge Gap Due to Attrition
  • Employee engagement, succession planning, contingent resource pool
  • Knowledge management to reduce manual dependency
