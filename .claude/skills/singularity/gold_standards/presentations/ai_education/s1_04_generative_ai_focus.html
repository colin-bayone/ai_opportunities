The Content Branch

Generative AI and
Language Models

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that creates new content based on instructions you give it. Text, images, code, audio, and video can all be generated from a prompt. Language models are the most widely adopted form, trained on massive amounts of text to understand and produce written language.

Unlike machine learning and computer vision, which work behind the scenes, generative AI is the first branch that most people interact with directly through a conversation. That is why it gets so much attention and why understanding its strengths and limitations matters.
