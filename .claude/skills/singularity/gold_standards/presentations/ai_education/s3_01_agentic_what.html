Agentic AI

Agentic AI is already here. These are systems that can take a goal, break it into steps, execute those steps, evaluate the results, and adjust course with minimal human intervention. The distinction from traditional AI is not intelligence. It is autonomy within constraints.

Traditional Software

Rigid, pre-programmed

Follows fixed rules that a developer wrote in advance. If X happens, do Y. No flexibility, no adaptation, no ability to handle situations that were not explicitly coded for.

A form that rejects your input because you put a dash in your phone number. The system was told to expect 10 digits, and it has no way to interpret anything else.

Language Models

Flexible, but passive

Responds to prompts one at a time. You ask a question, you get an answer. You give an instruction, you get an output. The human drives every step of the process.

You paste a document into ChatGPT and ask it to summarize. You read the summary. You copy it into an email. You edit the email. You send it. Every step is manual.

Agentic AI

Flexible and proactive

Chains multiple steps together, uses tools, and makes intermediate decisions within boundaries you define. The human sets the goal and the guardrails, and the system executes.

You say "review this client's quarterly portfolio, compare it to their plan, draft a summary of deviations, and prepare an email to schedule a review meeting." It does all of that and holds everything for your approval.
