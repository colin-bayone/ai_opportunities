## Pradeep -- Action-Item Contact

### The Ambiguity
The transcript has Anuj say: "Ask Pradeep what you want, right? Very crystal clear. I will work with him to try and get information back to you by tomorrow."

This is notable because the known BayOne contact who participated in the discovery call is **Pratik Sharda**, referred to as "Pat" throughout the meeting transcript. The question: is "Pradeep" a different person, or is this a transcription error for "Pratik"?

### Evidence It Could Be the Same Person as Pat/Pratik
- Anuj says he will "work with him" to get information to Colin -- suggesting this person has a direct line to the Lam team, which Pat/Pratik does.
- Anuj earlier says "drop in an email to Pat" and then shortly after says "ask Pradeep what you want." In context, these could be the same instruction about the same person, with the speech-to-text engine mangling "Pratik" as "Pradeep."
- No "Pradeep" appears anywhere else in the engagement materials.

### Evidence It Could Be a Different Person
- "Pradeep" and "Pratik" are distinct Indian names -- not obvious homonyms.
- Anuj says "I will work with him" about Pradeep but separately tells Colin to "drop in an email to Pat" -- potentially two different instructions to two different people.
- It is plausible that Pradeep is someone on the BayOne delivery side (perhaps replacing or supplementing Surej KP) who would handle the information-gathering legwork.

### Assessment
- **Most likely:** "Pradeep" is a transcription artifact for "Pratik" (Pat). The conversational flow treats the "email to Pat" and "ask Pradeep" as the same action item. Speech-to-text frequently confuses similar-sounding names.
- **Flag:** Cannot confirm with certainty. If a distinct "Pradeep" exists on the BayOne team, he has not appeared in any other source material.
