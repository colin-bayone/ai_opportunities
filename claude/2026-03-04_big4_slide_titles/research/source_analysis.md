# Source Analysis: Slide Title Context

## BayOne's Existing Title Patterns

From the capabilities deck, BayOne uses these title structures:

**Pattern 1: The [Concept] [Noun]**
- "The AI Execution Gap"

**Pattern 2: [Noun Phrase]**
- "Our Approach"
- "Solution Overview"
- "Technology Foundation"
- "Client Portfolio"
- "Engagement Model"

**Pattern 3: [Domain] + [Type]**
- "Developer Productivity"
- "Enterprise Automation"
- "Document Intelligence"
- "Data & Analytics Intelligence"
- "Applied AI & Intelligent Systems"

**Pattern 4: Why [Company]**
- "Why BayOne"

## Key Observations

1. BayOne titles are SHORT (2-4 words max)
2. No verbs in titles - all noun phrases
3. No "AI for X" pattern used in existing deck
4. Titles describe the DOMAIN, not the benefit
5. "Intelligence" appears twice as a professional alternative to "AI"
6. Compound phrases use "&" (Data & Analytics, Applied AI & Intelligent Systems)
