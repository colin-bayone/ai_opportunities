# Slide 6 - Visual Elements

This slide contains several distinct visual elements:

1.  **World Map**

    *   **Position:** Center of the slide.

    *   **Text:** None.

2.  **Location Pointers and Labels**
    This is a system of labels and pointers indicating geographic areas of operation.

    *   **North America Group (Magenta):**

        *   **Position:** Left side of the slide.

    *   **Latam Group (Purple):**
        *   **Type:** Diagram (Pointer with labels)
        *   **Position:** Center-left of the slide.
        *   **Description:** A single purple pointer line originates from the "Latam" text box at the bottom, extending to a purple oval label over Central America. A separate, unconnected purple rounded rectangle label is placed over South America.
        *   **Text:** `CRI` (in the oval), `LATAM` (in the rectangle).

    *   **APJC/EMEAR Group (Purple):**
        *   **Type:** Diagram (Pointer with labels)
        *   **Position:** Right and center of the slide.
        *   **Description:** A single long purple pointer originates from the "APJC" text box at the bottom, extending leftwards to a purple circular label over India. Two separate, unconnected purple rounded rectangle labels are placed over Asia/Pacific and Europe/Middle East respectively.
        *   **Text:** `IND` (in the circle), `APJC` (over Asia/Pacific), `EMEAR` (over Europe/Middle East).

3.  **Information Boxes**

5.  **Company Logo and Tagline**
