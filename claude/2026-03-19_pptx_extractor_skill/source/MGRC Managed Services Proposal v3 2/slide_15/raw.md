# Proposed Discovery Sprint — BayOne Investment

BayOne proposes a 3-5 day on-site discovery sprint at McGrath Livermore — at our investment — to validate scope, baseline complexity, and de-risk the engagement before contract finalization.

---
**Day 1-2**
## Deep Dive
System architecture walkthroughs with MGRC technical leads. Integration inventory validation (MuleSoft/OIC). Document undocumented customizations — the #1 risk identified.

---
**Day 3**
## Process Mapping
Shadow current support workflows. Map ticket routing logic. Identify automation quick wins for FreshService and integration monitoring.

---
**Day 4-5**
## Readout & Baseline
Present findings to Garni's team. Deliver refined staffing model based on actual complexity. Propose phased SLA targets grounded in data, not assumptions.

---
**Why this matters:** No other vendor will invest their own time to validate scope before asking MGRC to commit. This sprint replaces guesswork with data — and demonstrates BayOne's confidence in our embedded advantage.

---
