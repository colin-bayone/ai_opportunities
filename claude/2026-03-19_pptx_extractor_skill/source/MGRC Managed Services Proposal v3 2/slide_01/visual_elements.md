# Slide 1 - Visual Elements

This slide contains three primary visual elements: two company logos in the main body and a decorative footer bar.

1.  **BAYONE Logo**
    -   **Type:** Logo
    -   **Position:** Center-left
    -   **Description:** The logo consists of the word "BAYONE" in a black, sans-serif font. The letter "O" is a stylized graphic made of two overlapping circles, one magenta and one purple. Below the name is the tagline "#TheFutureWorksHere." in a smaller, gray, sans-serif font.
    -   **Text:**
        -   BAYONE
        -   #TheFutureWorksHere.

2.  **McGRATH Logo**
    -   **Type:** Logo
    -   **Position:** Center-right
    -   **Description:** The word "McGRATH" presented in a purple, serif font, with all letters capitalized.
    -   **Text:** McGRATH

3.  **Footer Bar**
    -   **Type:** Graphic element
    -   **Position:** Bottom of the slide
    -   **Description:** A full-width decorative bar. The base is a light lavender color, with translucent, wavy layers of darker purple and magenta on top, most prominent on the right side. On the far left of this bar, the BAYONE logo and tagline are displayed in white font.
    -   **Text:** BAYONE #TheFutureWorksHere.
