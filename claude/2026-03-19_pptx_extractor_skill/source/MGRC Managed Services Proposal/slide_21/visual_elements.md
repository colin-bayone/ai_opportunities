# Slide 21 - Visual Elements

- **Type:** Table
- **Position on slide:** Center, occupying the majority of the slide's content area.
- **Description:** A two-column, eight-row table (including the header row). The header row has a solid purple background with white text. The subsequent rows have a light, off-white background with dark grey text, separated by thin, light grey horizontal lines. The table outlines various risks and their corresponding mitigation strategies.

- **Position on slide:** Bottom-left, inside the decorative banner.

The "O" in "BAYONE" is replaced by a stylized circle composed of four colored segments (purple, lavender, and shades of pink). To the right of the logo is the tagline "#TheFutureWorksHere." in a lighter grey, sans-serif font.

- **Type:** Decorative Graphic / Banner
- **Position on slide:** Bottom edge.
- **Description:** A footer banner composed of abstract, overlapping wavy shapes. The colors are shades of light purple, lavender, and magenta, creating a soft, layered effect. The company logo and tagline are placed on top of this banner.

- **Type:** Line Separator
- **Position on slide:** Top, directly underneath the main title.
- **Description:** A thin, solid horizontal line in a magenta color that spans most of the width of the content area. It serves to visually separate the title from the table below.
