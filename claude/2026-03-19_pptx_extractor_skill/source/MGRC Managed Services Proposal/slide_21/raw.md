# Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **KT & Documentation Availability** | McGrath facilitation of KT plan. BayOne embedded team reduces dependency on incumbent. |
| **Infrastructure Support** | Infrastructure teams provide environment access, system accesses & approvals. |
| **Information Unavailability** | All information shared by incumbent in timely manner. Delays may impact key SLAs. |
| **Delayed Sign-offs** | Sign off entry/exit criteria. All necessary approvals, software, and timely accesses. |
| **Ticket Volume Uncertainty** | Discovery phase, robust KA plan, shadow & reverse shadow phases. Leveraging tools & automation. |
| **Geopolitical Scenarios** | BayOne operates from USA, Canada, Philippines, Costa Rica & India. Continues global growth. |
| **Knowledge Gap / Attrition** | Employee engagement, succession planning, contingent resource pool, knowledge management. |
