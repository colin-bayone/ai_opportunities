# Assumptions

- Monthly ticket volume for L2/L3 support: ~1,400. Complexity ratio: 30% simple, 50% medium, 20% complex
- Resolution efforts: Simple 1 hr, Medium 3 hrs, Complex 8 hrs
- P1/P2 tickets volume < 10%, P3/P4 > 90%
- On-boarding / discovery phase used to baseline data. Deviations necessitate a change request
- All functional stakeholders present during discovery phase (Phase 1)
- Resource count may be revised if ticket count deviates ±10% or integration count differs from RFP
- Most MuleSoft integrations will migrate to OIC
- Production replica environment available to simulate & recreate production issues
- Access and escalation coordination with third-party vendors
- Structured knowledge transfer sessions and handover documentation from incumbent
- Availability of ticketing tools, documentation repositories, and communication channels
- COLA (Cost of Living Adjustment) as applicable

BAYONE #TheFutureWorksHere.
