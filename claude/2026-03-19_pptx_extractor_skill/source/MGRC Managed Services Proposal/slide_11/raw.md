# BayOne Escalation Matrix

A structured, multi-level escalation framework ensures rapid issue resolution, clear accountability and proactive risk mitigation.

| Level | Escalate To | Possible Triggers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Project Manager<br>Program Manager | SLA breach risk, recurring defects, minor timeline delays, resource constraints |
| **Level 2** | Delivery Manager<br>cc: Account Manager | SLA breach, high priority issues not resolved, quality issues, high defect rates |
| **Level 3** | Account Manager, DM<br>cc: US Sales Head, CTO | Customer dissatisfaction, formal complaints, contractual risks, commercial decisions |
| **Level 4** | CEO, CTO<br>cc: AM, DM, US Sales Head | Strategic account risk, legal/compliance issues, significant financial impact |
