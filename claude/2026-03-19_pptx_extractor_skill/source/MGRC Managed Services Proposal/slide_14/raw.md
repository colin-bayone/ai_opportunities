---

### 1
**Preliminary**
Week 1-2

Kick-off meeting, access to infrastructure, documentation review, risk assessment

---

### 2
**Knowledge Acquisition**
Week 3-6

Current implementation assessment, system architecture review, integration assessment, data flow overview, test case execution

---

### 3
**Shadow Support**
Week 7-10

Attend meetings & calls with stakeholders, document observed processes, identify areas for additional training

---

### 4
**Reverse Shadow & Ownership**
Week 11-13

Handle live tickets with incumbent supervision, receive feedback, ensure SLA adherence, knowledge validation
