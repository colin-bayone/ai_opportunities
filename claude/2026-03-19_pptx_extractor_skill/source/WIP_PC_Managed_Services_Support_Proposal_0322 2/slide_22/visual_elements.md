# Slide 22 - Visual Elements

1.  **Person Icons**
    *   **Type:** Icon
    *   **Position:** Within each of the four main columns, in sections labeled "Onsite team size" and "Offshore team size".
    *   **Description:** Simple, solid blue stylized icons of human figures. The "Onsite" sections show two single-person icons with ellipses between them, implying more. The "Offshore" sections show two icons of a group of three people. These icons visually represent the team members.

2.  **Team Size Boxes**
    *   **Type:** Diagram
    *   **Position:** Top half of the slide, organized into four columns under the Phase headers.
    *   **Description:** Four pairs of rounded-corner boxes, each containing person icons and a number representing the team size. The top row is for "Onsite team size" and the bottom row is for "Offshore team size".
    *   **Text:**
        *   Phase 1, Q1: Onsite 11, Offshore 12
        *   Phase 2, Y1 Q1-Q4: Onsite 11, Offshore 26
        *   Phase 2, Y2 Q1-Q4: Onsite 7, Offshore 24
        *   Phase 2, Y3 Q1-Q4: Onsite 4, Offshore 18

3.  **Stacked Bar Charts**
    *   **Type:** Chart
    *   **Position:** Below the team and ticket information for each of the three "Phase 2" time periods.
    *   **Description:** Three vertical stacked bar charts illustrating the ratio of "Support" to "Enhancement" efforts. Each chart has three sections: a large dark blue section at the bottom ("Enhancement"), a smaller light blue section in the middle ("Support"), and a small pink section at the very top ("Burst capacity").
    *   **Text (Data labels on charts):**
        *   Chart 1 (Y1): `10%` (light blue), `90%` (dark blue)
        *   Chart 2 (Y2): `15%` (light blue), `85%` (dark blue)
        *   Chart 3 (Y3): `30%` (light blue), `70%` (dark blue)

4.  **Chart Legend**
    *   **Type:** Legend
    *   **Position:** Bottom-right, to the right of the "Key Callout" section.
    *   **Description:** Three colored squares, each with a corresponding text label to explain the colors used in the stacked bar charts.
    *   **Text:**
        *   A light teal/blue square next to "Support"
        *   A dark blue square next to "Enhancement"
        *   A light pink square next to "Burst capacity"

5.  **BAYONE Logo**
    *   **Type:** Logo
    *   **Position:** Bottom-left corner of the slide.
    *   **Description:** The word "BAYONE" in a bold, capitalized, sans-serif font. To its right is the hashtag "#TheFutureWorksHere." in a lighter weight font.
    *   **Text:** BAYONE #TheFutureWorksHere.

6.  **Header and Footer Bands**
    *   **Type:** Graphic / Background Element
    *   **Position:** A solid purple band at the top, and a purple-to-lavender gradient wave at the bottom.
    *   **Description:** These are decorative elements that frame the slide's content. The top band sits behind the main title. The bottom band sits behind the Key Callouts, legend, and logo.
