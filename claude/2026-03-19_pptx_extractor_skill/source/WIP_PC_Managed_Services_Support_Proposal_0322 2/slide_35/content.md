**Case Study- Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM Managed Services supporte**
***

**Business Scenario**
Post successful implementation of Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM, a large-scale national security agency required **long-term managed services support** to ensure system stability, handle evolving HR needs, and manage Oracle's quarterly updates—allowing internal teams to focus on strategic initiatives

***

**Scope, Services & Technologies**
*   **Scope:** Incident management, quarterly updates, enhancements, security & compliance, user support, performance optimization
*   **Services:** Break-fix support, regression testing, audit support, training, system monitoring
*   **Technology:** Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM (Core HR, Payroll, Absence, Learning, Talent, Analytics)

***

**Solution**
*   Established structured AMS with **SLA-driven incident management (P1-P4)**
*   Managed **end-to-end quarterly updates** (impact analysis, regression testing, defect resolution)
*   Delivered **functional enhancements across modules**
*   Implemented **security governance (SoD, audits, patching)**
*   Enabled **knowledge base, training, and performance optimization initiative**

***

**Benefits**
*   **95.7% SLA compliance** with **100% P1** adherence
*   **Zero payroll failures** and **99.8% system uptime**
*   Improved performance (e.g., payroll processing reduced from 4.5 hrs to 1.8 hrs)
*   **87% user satisfaction** and continuous enhancement delivery
*   Proactive update management preventing production issue
