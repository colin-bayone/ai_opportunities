-   **Position:** In the "Trend" column of the three tables at the top of the slide.

-   **Position:** Bottom-left corner of the slide.

-   **Position:** Top of the slide, below the main title.

-   **Type:** Decorative Background Graphic
-   **Position:** Bottom of the slide, behind the logo and page number.
-   **Description:** An abstract graphic composed of large, overlapping, translucent curves in shades of light purple and pink, creating a soft, layered background effect.

-   **Position:** Used as headers for all tables and sections.
