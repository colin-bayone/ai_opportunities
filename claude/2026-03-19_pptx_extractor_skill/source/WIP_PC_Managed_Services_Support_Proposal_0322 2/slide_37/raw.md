# Case Study - Oracle EBS support
## Managed Service

---
### Business Scenario
A global leader in materials, networking, and laser technologies across industrial, communications, electronics, and instrumentation sectors partnered with BayOne Solutions to support day-to-day business operations across more than 30 global sites. BayOne has served as a long-term technology partner for over 12 years, providing ongoing operational support and guiding the enterprise through its digital transformation from Oracle on-premise systems to modern Cloud-based applications.

---

| Services | Projects Delivered |
| :--- | :--- |
| <ul><li>Day To Day Operations support on Oracle eBS 11i & R12.</li><li>Upgradation of 11i to R12.2.10</li><li>Merger of Legacy Systems on Oracle Unified instance (EBS & Fusion) for acquired companies. BayOne has provided support on functional, **technical** and Database areas.</li><li>Supporting **Finance, EPM, HCM, Procurement, Manufacturing, WMS, Transportation & Field service modules** and peripheral integrations / applications.</li><li>Managing **monthly / quarterly upgrades, regression testing and smoke testing**</li><li>Advising and **Implementing new features** released with monthly/ quarterly upgrades.</li><li>Monitoring of Critical Jobs (Command Centre Set up).</li><li>**Design Development & Facilitate the Oracle BIP reporting on EBS & Fusion both as well as QuickView (Qview).**</li><li>Manage the **Month-End Activities**</li><li>Partner on **Oracle Cloud Transformation journey**</li></ul> | <ul><li>Data Migration during M&A</li><li>Functional & Technical activities during the migration & merger of sites all over the globe</li><li>Technical Integration with Kardex with Oracle eBS for Germany</li><li>Procurement Approval Hierarchies based on new MOA rules using AME – CAR Project</li><li>Supporting Global Sites with 90% loading in India</li><li>Export compliance Implementation for different Oracle system</li></ul> |
