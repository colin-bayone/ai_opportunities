# Vulnerability Management Support
<br>
**Managed Service**

---

### Business Scenario
*   Collaborating with the SRE & DevOps team for fixing the vulnerability issues & misconfigurations due to server upgrade, patches, new capabilities or feature

### Scope, Services & Technologies
*   Support & manage
    *   Triaging vulnerability issues arising as part of GLCP initiative
    *   Incident management (L1 support)
    *   Reporting & ownership of closing these issues
    *   Process documentation
*   Team Size: 10 (2 Onsite + 8 Offshore)

| Solution | Benefits |
| :--- | :--- |
| *   Daily report creation from wiz to provide visibility into vulnerability issues<br>*   Triaging the critical (P0 / P1) incidents to SRE & DevOps team for fixing<br>*   Documenting the end-to-end process<br>*   Jira ticket creation corresponding to each issue & assign to the correct owner | *   **Enhanced efficiency** - team's ability to manage more projects for vulnerability issues triaging with the same bandwidth<br>*   **Automation** to reduce manual intervention |
