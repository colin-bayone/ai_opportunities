# Slide 24 - Visual Elements

This slide contains several visual elements designed to structure the content and provide branding.

1.  **Type:** Graphic Line
    *   **Position:** Top-center, directly below the main title.
    *   **Description:** A solid, thick horizontal line in a magenta/purple color. It visually separates the title from the main content of the slide.

2.  **Type:** Content Boxes
    *   **Position:** The main body of the slide is organized into a three-column, two-row grid of boxes.
    *   **Description:** The slide uses rectangular boxes to group related information. The top row of boxes and the bottom horizontal banner have solid purple headers/backgrounds with white text. The boxes in the second row have a very light lavender background with black text and bold black headers. This creates a clear visual hierarchy for the phased approach.

3.  **Type:** Logo
    *   **Position:** Bottom-left corner.
    *   **Description:** The logo for "BAYONE". The text "BAYONE" is in a bold, black, sans-serif font.
    *   **Text:** BAYONE

4.  **Type:** Hashtag Text
    *   **Position:** Bottom-left corner, to the right of the BAYONE logo.
    *   **Description:** A text-based graphic element presented as a hashtag. The text is gray.
    *   **Text:** #TheFutureWorksHere.

5.  **Type:** Decorative Graphic
    *   **Position:** Bottom of the slide, spanning the full width.
    *   **Description:** A fluid, wavy graphic composed of overlapping shapes in shades of purple, lavender, and pink. It serves as a decorative footer for the slide.
