# Slide 16 - Visual Elements

### **Diagrams**
- **Type:** Governance Model Diagram
- **Position:** The main content of the slide.
- **Description:** A three-tiered flowchart-style diagram illustrating the governance structure between two entities, "McGrath" and "BayOne". The structure is divided into Strategic, Tactical, and Operational levels. Arrows indicate the frequency of interaction at each level and a central vertical arrow represents overall communication. The diagram connects roles and teams at each level to a corresponding list of reporting topics.

- **Type:** Organizational Chart Diagram
- **Position:** Center column ("BayOne"), bottom section ("Operational").
- **Description:** A small hierarchy diagram showing "Leads / Mgrs" at the top, branching down to three groups of icons representing the "BayOne support team".
