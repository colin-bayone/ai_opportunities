# Sample Profiles - Mulesoft

A results-driven Technical Lead with 13+ years of experience delivering enterprise-grade MuleSoft integrations, API-led architectures, and cloud-based data engineering solutions. Proven track record leading cross-functional teams and modernizing legacy systems for large enterprises in energy, healthcare, banking, and technology.
