# Case Study - Accelerating Reporting for Oracle Fusion ERP
Managed Service
***

## Business Scenario
Global manufacturing enterprise implemented **Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP** across Finance and Supply Chain. While the platform provided standard analytics, business stakeholders required custom operational and statutory reports for reconciliation, regulatory reporting, and operational decision making. Challenges included:
*   Limited visibility into supplier liabilities & customer receivables
*   Lack of consolidated inventory and procurement insights

## Solution Approach
Our team designed and deployed 25+ custom BI Publisher reports across Finance and SCM modules. The solution included:
*   Custom BIP data models and SQL queries
*   Business-friendly RTF report layouts
*   Automated report bursting and email distribution
*   Integration extracts for downstream systems

**Key report areas:**
*   **Finance:** AP Aging, AR Statements, Trial Balance, Budget vs Actual
*   **Procurement:** Open Purchase Orders, Supplier Spend
*   **Inventory:** Stock on Hand, Inventory Valuation
*   **Order Management:** Sales Order Status and

## Technical Architecture
ERP Data → BIP Data Model (SQL) → RTF Templates → Automated Bursting & Distribution

Capabilities delivered:
*   Automated report scheduling
*   Email bursting to stakeholders
*   Integration extracts for downstream systems
*   Finance reconciliation dashboards

## Business Outcomes & Impact
*   40% reduction in manual reconciliation effort
*   Real-time financial visibility across business units
*   Faster month-end closing cycles
*   Improved supplier and customer reporting transparency
*   Seamless integration reporting with SCM CRM and analytics tools

**Impact**
The enterprise now runs automated reporting across 25+ business-critical reports, enabling finance, procurement, and operations teams to make faster data-driven decisions.
