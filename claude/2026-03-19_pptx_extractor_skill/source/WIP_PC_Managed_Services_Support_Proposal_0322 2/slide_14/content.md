# Communication Model

**McGrath Management**
- Communicates "Service Requirements / Escalations" to BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr.
- Receives "Status Reports / Escalation updates" from BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr.

**McGrath Team**
- Communicates "Escalations" to BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr.
- Receives "Escalation Updates" from BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr.
- Communicates "Recommendations" to BayOne Support Engineers.
- Receives "Status" from BayOne Support Engineers.

**BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr**
- Receives "Service Requirements / Escalations" from McGrath Management.
- Communicates "Status Reports / Escalation updates" to McGrath Management.
- Receives "Escalations" from McGrath Team.
- Communicates "Escalation Updates" to McGrath Team.
- Communicates "General Guidance" to BayOne Support Engineers.
- Receives "Periodic Reports & Escalation Updates" from BayOne Support Engineers.

**BayOne Support Engineers**
- Receives "Recommendations" from McGrath Team.
- Communicates "Status" to McGrath Team.
- Receives "General Guidance" from BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr.
- Communicates "Periodic Reports & Escalation Updates" to BayOne SMEs / Lead / PgMgr.
