### Human Resources
Streamline talent operations from recruiting through retention with intelligent automation.

- **Recruiting Automation** with AI-powered candidate screening and pipeline management.
- **Onboarding Workflows** that automate document processing and new hire setup.
- **Policy Q&A Assistants** providing instant answers from HR documentation.
- **Workforce Analytics** for attrition prediction and skills gap analysis.

---

### Finance
Accelerate financial operations with intelligent document processing and predictive insights.

- **Expense Processing** with automated receipt extraction, policy validation, and approval routing.
- **Invoice Automation** for AP processing with intelligent matching and exception handling.
- **Financial Reporting** with automated reconciliation and close cycle acceleration.
- **Forecasting & Anomaly Detection** for cash flow prediction and fraud prevention.

### Legal
Transform contract operations and compliance workflows with intelligent review and monitoring.

- **Contract Review** with automated clause extraction, risk identification, and comparison.
- **Contract Lifecycle Management** from drafting through renewal with automated tracking.
- **Compliance Monitoring** for regulatory tracking, policy adherence, and risk assessment.
- **Legal Knowledge Search** across contracts, precedents, and regulatory guidance.

### Marketing
Scale content creation and campaign optimization with AI-powered marketing intelligence.

- **Content Generation** for campaigns, product descriptions, and social media at scale.
- **Personalization Engines** delivering tailored experiences across web and email.
- **Campaign Automation** with intelligent scheduling, A/B optimization, and performance prediction.
- **Marketing Analytics** with attribution modeling and ROI dashboards.
