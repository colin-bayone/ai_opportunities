# Slide 7 - Visual Elements

This slide contains a central diagram, several icons within it, a logo, and some minor graphic elements.

**1. Header Bar**
*   **Type:** Graphic element
*   **Position:** Top of the slide.
*   **Description:** A decorative, rounded bar with a left-to-right gradient from purple to magenta. It sits behind the top left of the title "Our Key Differentiators".

**2. Hashtag Box**
*   **Type:** Graphic element
*   **Position:** Top-right.
*   **Description:** A light grey, rounded rectangle (pill shape) containing text.
*   **Text:** `#TheFutureWorksHere`

**3. Donut Chart Logo**
*   **Type:** Logo / Chart
*   **Position:** Top-right, to the right of the hashtag box.
*   **Description:** A circular donut chart or logo with three segments: a large purple segment, a medium magenta segment, and a small pink segment. It has a white center.

**4. Central Diagram**
*   **Type:** Diagram
*   **Position:** Center of the slide.
*   **Description:** A diagram illustrating key concepts. In the very center is the word "BayOne" in purple text. This is surrounded by six purple circles, arranged in a hexagon, each containing a white line-art icon. Each icon circle is connected to the center by a thin line and is positioned next to a grey, rounded text box that labels the concept.

**5. Icons within the Central Diagram**
*   **Type:** Icons
*   **Position:** Arranged in a hexagon in the center of the slide.
*   **Description:** Six white icons inside purple circles, each representing a key differentiator.
    *   **Business Partnership (Top):** An icon depicting a handshake at the bottom, a gear in the middle, and a person's profile with a magnifying glass over their eye at the top.
    *   **Team Build (Top-Right):** An icon showing the heads and shoulders of a group of five people.
    *   **Talent Management (Bottom-Right):** An icon showing a male symbol and a female symbol with a small bar chart between them.
    *   **Delivery Model (Bottom):** An icon with two circles, each containing a person symbol, connected by a central node, representing a network.
    *   **Performance Culture (Bottom-Left):** An icon of a vertical bar chart.
    *   **Technology & Innovation (Top-Left):** An icon of a large gear with a person's head and shoulders silhouetted inside it.

**6. Text Labels within the Central Diagram**
*   **Type:** Graphic element (text boxes)
*   **Position:** Around the central hexagon of icons.
*   **Description:** Six light grey, rounded rectangles, each containing the title for the adjacent icon.
*   **Text (clockwise from top):**
    *   Business Partnership
    *   Team Build
    *   Talent Management
    *   Delivery Model
    *   Performance Culture
    *   Technology & Innovation
