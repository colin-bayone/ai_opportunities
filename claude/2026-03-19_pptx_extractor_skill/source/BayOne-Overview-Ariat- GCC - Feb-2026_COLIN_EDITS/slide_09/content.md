### Community Cohorts
We launch Cohort Programs in collaboration with enterprises to provide women from underserved communities with months of technical training in AI and Software Engineering by expert instructors.

### Girl Empowerment
Partnering with Pleasanton Unified School District, we mentor middle & high school girls, offer apprenticeships to high schoolers, & provide scholarships to Girls Who Code, nurturing the next generation of female leaders in technology.

### BayOne Delivery
BayOne's female hires grew from 26% to 46% in four years, thanks to fair selection processes, implementing the "Rooney Rule," and an AI platform sourcing diverse candidates, showcasing our dedication to diversity.
