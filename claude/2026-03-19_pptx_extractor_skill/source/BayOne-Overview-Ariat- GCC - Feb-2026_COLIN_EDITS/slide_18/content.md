---

Overall, the BOT model offers minimal entry time, minimal risk with minimal exposure to ramping down resources; If required.

It enables “learning while doing” with full flexibility but does carry with it some transition risk.

This can be reduced by using a Joint Venture model which enables sharing the legal risk with the joint venture entity, while also postponing reward to the JV partner on account of a delayed compensation arising out of an exit agreement 3 to 5 years down the road.

In this model, the JV partner shares the risk and reward.
