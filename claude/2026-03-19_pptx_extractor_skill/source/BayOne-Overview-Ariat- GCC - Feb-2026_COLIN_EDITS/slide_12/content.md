# Next-Generation Quality Engineering
AI-powered acceleration across testing, analysis, and visibility

#TheFutureWorksHere

### **Test Case Clustering**
Group similar tests by structure and dependencies to process as families.

*   **Similarity Analysis** groups tests into manageable families.
*   **Pattern Application** applies one template across each family.
*   **Batch Processing** addresses multiple tests simultaneously.
*   **Reduced Manual Effort** through family-based automation.

### **Intelligent Routing**
Governance-driven playbook for routing decisions based on historical patterns.

*   **Governance Playbook** defines rules from historical allowances.
*   **Autonomy Levels** support full, partial, or semi-autonomous operation.
*   **Human-Controlled** decisions with configurable workflows.
*   **Industry Adaptable** rules by company and compliance.

### **Root Cause Analysis**
Failure correlation distinguishing primary issues from cascading symptoms.

*   **Dependency Mapping** traces code, test, and data relationships.
*   **Cascade Detection** identifies downstream failure triggers.
*   **Rapid Analysis** reduces hours to minutes.
*   **Impact Detection** identifies affected tests instantly.

### **Agentic Diagnosis**
AI deep dives into the codebase to diagnose, troubleshoot, and propose actions.

*   **Codebase Exploration** with AI agents navigating your code.
*   **Troubleshooting** surfaces causes and links to logs.
*   **Proposed Actions** with suggested fixes.
*   **Pattern Learning** improves accuracy over time.

### **Visibility Dashboard**
Single pane of glass across your build and deployment stack.

*   **Unified Interface** consolidates CI/CD and monitoring tools.
*   **Conversational Queries** like "Why did this fail?"
*   **Reporting Insights** with trends and metrics.
*   **Failure Attribution** identifies who to follow up with.

### **Automated Testing**
Playwright UI/UX testing with coverage tracking and regression analysis.

*   **Playwright UI/UX** for visual and interaction testing.
*   **Unit Test Coverage** at condition level.
*   **Regression Analysis** with smart test selection.
*   **Smart Selection** runs only relevant tests.
