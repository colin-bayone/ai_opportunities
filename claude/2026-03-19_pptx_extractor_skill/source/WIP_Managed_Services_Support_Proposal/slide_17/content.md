# BayOne Escalation Matrix

| Escalation Level | Escalate to (Role) | Possible Triggers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Project Manager<br>Program Manager | <ul><li>SLA breach risk</li><li>Recurring defects</li><li>Minor timelines delays</li><li>Resource constraints</li></ul> |
| **Level 2** | Delivery Manager<br>cc: Account Manager | <ul><li>SLA breach</li><li>High priority issues not resolved within SLAs</li><li>Quality issues</li><li>High defect rates</li></ul> |
| **Level 3** | Account Manager, Delivery Manager<br>cc: US Sales Head, CTO | <ul><li>Customer dissatisfaction</li><li>Formal complaints</li><li>Contractual risks</li><li>Commercial or scope decisions</li></ul> |
| **Level 4** | CEO, CTO<br>cc: Account Manager, Delivery Manager, US Sales Head | <ul><li>Strategic account risk</li><li>Legal or compliance issues</li><li>Significant financial impact</li></ul> |

A structured, multi-level escalation framework ensures rapid issue resolution, clear accountability and proactive risk mitigation across onsite and offshore teams.
