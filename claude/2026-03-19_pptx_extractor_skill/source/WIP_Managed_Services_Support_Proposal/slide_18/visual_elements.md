This slide contains a single complex diagram illustrating a governance and reporting structure, along with a title and a footer.

**1. Title Bar**
- **Type:** Title
- **Position:** Top, center.
- **Description:** Large, bold, black text on a white background, underlined by a thick magenta line.

**2. Governance & Reporting Diagram**
- **Type:** Flowchart / Organizational Diagram
- **Position:** Main body of the slide.
- **Description:** The diagram is organized into three main vertical columns that represent different entities and topics, and three horizontal rows that represent different levels of engagement (Strategic, Tactical, Operational).

    - **McGrath Column (Left):**
        - **Description:** A column with a dark grey header labeled "McGrath". It contains three rounded light grey boxes, one for each level. Each box has a dark grey vertical bar on its left.

        - **Tactical Box:** Contains an icon of a single male figure in a suit and tie. Text: "Tactical" (vertical), "Manager". An arrow points from this box to the BayOne Tactical box with the label "Monthly".
        - **Operational Box:** Contains an icon of two figures (male and female) in business attire. Text: "Operational" (vertical), "McGrath Team". An arrow points from this box to the BayOne Operational box with the labels "Daily" and "Weekly".

    - **BayOne Column (Center):**
        - **Description:** A column with a purple header labeled "BayOne". It contains three rounded light purple boxes, one for each level. Each box has a dark purple vertical bar on its left.
        - **Strategic Box:** Contains two icons of male figures in suits. Text: "Strategic" (vertical), "Business Relationship Manager", "Delivery Manager".
        - **Tactical Box:** Contains three icons of male figures in suits. Text: "Tactical" (vertical), "Program Manager", "Project Manager", "Delivery Manager".
        - **Operational Box:** Depicts a small organizational chart. At the top is an icon of two figures (male and female) labeled "Leads / Mgrs". Lines connect this to three groups of icons below, representing teams, labeled "BayOne support team". Text: "Operational" (vertical).

    - **Communication Bar (Center-Right):**
        - **Description:** A thick, vertical, solid purple bar positioned between the BayOne column and the reporting topics. It has an arrow pointing upwards at the top and another arrow pointing downwards at the bottom, symbolizing two-way communication.
        - **Text:** "Communication" written vertically in white text.

    - **Reporting Topics Column (Right):**
        - **Description:** A series of rounded light grey rectangular boxes, grouped into three sections corresponding to the Strategic, Tactical, and Operational levels. Arrows point from each of the BayOne levels to the corresponding group of topics.
