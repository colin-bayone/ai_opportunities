# Slide 22 - Visual Elements

This slide contains several visual elements to illustrate a four-stage operational plan.

1.  **Phase Diagram/Timeline:**
    *   **Type:** Diagram
    *   **Position:** Top half of the slide.
    *   **Description:** A four-column layout representing a progression through time. Each column details a specific phase: "On-board", "Stabilize", "Optimize", and "Steady State". Each phase has associated timelines, team sizes, and workload metrics. The columns are separated by thin purple lines.

2.  **Team Size Icons:**
    *   **Type:** Icons
    *   **Position:** Within each of the four phase columns.
    *   **Description:** Two rows of blue, stylized icons of human figures are used in each column to represent "Onsite" and "Offshore" team sizes. The top row shows three figures with an ellipsis (...) to represent the onsite team. The bottom row uses two groups of three figures to represent the offshore team. A large number is written below each set of icons indicating the team size.

3.  **Stacked Bar Charts:**
    *   **Type:** Chart
    *   **Position:** Centered at the bottom of the "Stabilize", "Optimize", and "Steady State" columns.
    *   **Description:** Three vertical stacked bar charts illustrate the "Support vs Enhancements efforts" ratio for each phase.
        *   **Stabilize Chart:** Shows a small light blue section at the bottom (for Support), a large dark blue section in the middle labeled "90%" (for Enhancements), and a small pink section at the top (for Burst). The light blue section is labeled "10%".
        *   **Optimize Chart:** Shows a light blue section at the bottom, a large dark blue section labeled "85%" in the middle, and a pink section at the top. The light blue section is labeled "15%".
        *   **Steady State Chart:** Shows a light blue section at the bottom, a dark blue section labeled "70%" in the middle, and a pink section at the top. The light blue section is labeled "30%".
    The charts visually represent a shift from enhancement-heavy work towards a more balanced support and enhancement model over time.

4.  **Color Legend:**
    *   **Type:** Diagram/Legend
    *   **Position:** Bottom-right, to the right of the Key Callouts.
    *   **Description:** A key for the colors used in the bar charts. It consists of three colored squares, each with a corresponding label:
        *   A light blue square next to "Support capacity".
        *   A dark blue square next to "Enhancement capacity".
        *   A light pink square next to "Burst capacity".

5.  **Logo and Tagline:**
    *   **Type:** Logo/Text
    *   **Position:** Bottom-left corner.
    *   **Description:** The company name "BAYONE" is in a bold, dark gray sans-serif font, followed by the hashtag "#TheFutureWorksHere." in a lighter gray.

6.  **Decorative Graphic:**
    *   **Type:** Graphic Element
    *   **Position:** Bottom of the slide, behind the logo and page number.
    *   **Description:** A subtle, abstract wave-like graphic composed of overlapping shapes in shades of purple and pink.

7.  **Page Number:**
    *   **Type:** Text
    *   **Position:** Bottom-right corner.
    *   **Description:** The number "22", indicating the slide number.
