# Make Tech Purple Initiative

#MakeTechPurple is dedicated to bridging the gender divide in tech through dynamic upskilling and reskilling programs, ensuring women secure and thrive in lasting tech careers.

| Community Cohorts | Girl Empowerment | BayOne Delivery |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| We launch Cohort Programs in collaboration with enterprises to provide 10-15 women from underserved communities with 1-3 months of pro-bono technical training in AI and Software Engineering by expert instructors. | Partnering with Pleasanton Unified School District, we mentor middle & high school girls, offer apprenticeships to high schoolers, & provide scholarships to Girls Who Code, nurturing the next generation of female leaders in technology. | BayOne's female hires grew from 26% to 46% in four years, thanks to fair selection processes, implementing the "Rooney Rule," and an AI platform sourcing diverse candidates, showcasing our dedication to diversity. |
