# Assumptions
**WIP**

*   In the absence of ticket volume, it is assumed –
    *   Monthly ticket volume for L2 / L3 support: ~1400,
    *   Complexity ratio – 30% simple, 50% medium & 20% complex
    *   Resolution efforts: Simple: 1 Hr, Medium: 3 Hrs & Complex: 8 Hrs
    *   P1 / P2 tickets volume < 10%, P3 / P4 > 90%
*   On-boarding / discovery phase will be used to baseline the data. Any deviations from above will necessitate a change request
*   All the functional stakeholders will be present during the discovery phase (Phase 1)
*   Actual resource count may be revised if –
    *   ticket count deviates +/- 10% as specified
    *   Number of integrations differs from specified in the RFP document
    *   Most of the Mulesoft integration will migrate to OIC
*   COLA
*   Production replica environment is available to simulate & recreate production issues
*   Access and escalation coordination with third-party vendors supporting integrations or add-ons
*   Structured knowledge transfer sessions and handover documentation from incumbent/implementation team
*   Availability of ticketing tools, up to date documentation repositories and communication channels
