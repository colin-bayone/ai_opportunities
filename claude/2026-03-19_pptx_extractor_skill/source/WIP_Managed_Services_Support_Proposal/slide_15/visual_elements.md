# Slide 15 - Visual Elements

This slide contains a single, complex process flowchart and several textual and decorative elements.

**1. Diagram: Process Flowchart**
- **Type:** Diagram (Flowchart)
- **Position:** Center of the slide, occupying most of the space.
- **Description:** A detailed flowchart illustrating a high-level support process. It is divided into two main sections, "L0 / L1 Flow" and "L2 / L3 Flow", separated by a horizontal dashed line. The flowchart uses standard shapes: rounded rectangles for start/end points, a parallelogram for input, rectangles for processes, diamonds for decisions, and cylinders for databases. Shapes are color-coded: grey for the "McGrathTeam" (L0/L1) and purple for the "BayOne Team" (L2/L3). Arrows indicate the flow of the process, with some labeled "Yes" or "No".
- **Text Content (within shapes):**
    - **L0 / L1 Flow (Grey Shapes):**
        - Rounded Rectangle: "Start"
        - Parallelogram: "User support email / VM"
        - Rectangle: "Create Fresh Service ticket"
        - Diamond: "Is this supported ticket"
        - Rectangle: "Inform user"
        - Rounded Rectangle: "Close Ticket"
    - **L2 / L3 Flow (Purple Shapes):**
        - Rectangle: "Acknowledge user"
        - Diamond: "Can you resolve?"
        - Rectangle: "Assign ticket to yourself & resolve"
        - Rounded Rectangle: "Close Ticket"
        - Rectangle: "Assign to an agent who can resolve"
        - Diamond: "Can other agent resolve?"
        - Rectangle: "Assign to L3 team"
        - Rounded Rectangle: "Resolve & Close Ticket" (appears twice)
        - Cylinder: "Update Knowledge DB" (appears twice)
        - Rectangle: "Co-ordinate with 3rd party"
