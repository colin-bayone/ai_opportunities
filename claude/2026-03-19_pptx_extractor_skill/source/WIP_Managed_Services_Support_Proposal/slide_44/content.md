# Mom Relaunch Program

**<u>Mission:</u>**
To help women, looking to re-enter the workforce, level up in terms of skillset & placing them at our client's sites

**<u>Case Study:</u>**
Archana took a career break after starting a family and was looking to re-enter the workforce. She found it difficult to find a job as her skillset and experience were outdated.

With **BayOne's Mom Relaunch Program** under **Make Tech Purple** initiative, Archana was one of the many women we helped train in Salesforce Developer skills.
Since Archana was an excellent person to work with, BayOne hired her internally as we were switching over to a new CRM software and then eventually got her inducted into customer project
