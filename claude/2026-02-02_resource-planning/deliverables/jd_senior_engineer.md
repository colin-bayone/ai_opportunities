# Senior Solutions Engineer

**BayOne Solutions | On-site (San Jose, CA)**

## Position Overview

BayOne Solutions is hiring a Senior Solutions Engineer to lead technical delivery for a major enterprise client engagement. You will serve as the on-site technical lead, working directly with client engineering teams while coordinating an offshore development team. This is a hands-on role where you will write code, solve problems, and drive technical decisions daily.

The work focuses on improving CI/CD infrastructure, building internal tooling, and implementing AI-assisted automation for a Fortune 500 technology company.

## Key Responsibilities

### Hands-On Implementation (60%)

- Design and build data pipelines, backend services, and internal tools in Python
- Integrate with CI/CD platforms including Jenkins and Apache Airflow
- Write production-quality code with appropriate testing and documentation
- Troubleshoot issues across the stack and resolve technical blockers
- Prototype solutions and validate technical approaches before full implementation

### Technical Leadership (25%)

- Define technical architecture and implementation approach
- Coordinate daily with an offshore team of 4-5 engineers across backend, AI, and frontend specializations
- Conduct code reviews and ensure quality standards are maintained
- Break down complex requirements into clear, actionable tasks
- Report progress, risks, and blockers to BayOne and client leadership

### Client Engagement (15%)

- Serve as BayOne's primary technical point of contact with the client
- Participate in requirements gathering, sprint planning, and stakeholder meetings
- Navigate client systems to obtain necessary access, documentation, and approvals
- Build productive working relationships with client engineers and managers

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python, including data processing and API development
- Experience with CI/CD systems and DevOps tooling (Jenkins, Airflow, or equivalent)
- Demonstrated ability to lead technical projects or small engineering teams
- Experience working in consulting, professional services, or client-facing engineering roles
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience

## Preferred Qualifications

- Practical experience integrating AI/ML capabilities into production systems, particularly LLM-based tools and automation
- Familiarity with agentic AI concepts and patterns
- Experience with LangChain, LangGraph, or similar frameworks
- Direct API integration experience
- Proficiency with AI-assisted development tools (Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot, or similar)
- Hands-on experience with DevOps workflow automation and pipeline optimization
- Background in developer productivity, internal tooling, or platform engineering
- Experience coordinating distributed or offshore engineering teams
- Deep expertise in Python for backend services, data pipelines, and automation
- Strong Git and GitHub workflow experience
- Experience with Docker and containerized environments
- Working knowledge of SQL databases and Grafana
- Experience working in on-prem deployment environments
- Prior experience with Apache Airflow is a plus

Candidates with leadership experience in AI/ML integration or DevOps automation will be strongly considered.
