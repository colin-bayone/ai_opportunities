  challenges:

  Angular component selectors)
  - Lifecycle hooks map but are not identical

  - Pub/sub events need redesign for Angular's component architecture
  - Module system shifts from AMD to ES6 imports

  widgets. The hard part is understanding the behavior embedded in each Dojo
