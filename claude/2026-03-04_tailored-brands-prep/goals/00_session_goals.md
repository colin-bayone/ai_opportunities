# Session Goals

## Primary Objective
Prepare Colin for discovery meeting with Tailored Brands leadership where the goal is to **listen and understand their problems**, not pitch.

## Deliverables
1. **Transcript Decomposition** - Full analysis of internal planning meeting
2. **Talking Points Document** - HTML file with key questions, context, relationship map
3. **Research Brief** - Company background, strategic initiatives, competitive landscape

## Key Success Criteria
- Colin understands the full org structure and relationships
- Colin has context on what BayOne already knows vs. needs to discover
- Colin has prepared questions that demonstrate understanding without appearing to "tell them what they don't know"
- Document follows BayOne design system (like Sephora/Cisco examples)

## Approach Philosophy (from internal meeting)
- Listen and brainstorm, don't pitch
- Go light on case studies
- Stroke egos carefully
- Let them share first
- Break proposals into small pieces
- Ask questions that show curiosity, not expertise superiority
