Changelog

v2 — Semantic Shapes: Nodes now use shapes that convey their function (cylinders for storage, hexagons for orchestrators, diamonds for decisions, rounded for AI components).

  • NXMAIN, NXDEV: Cylinder shape (repositories / data stores)
  • JENKINS: Hexagon shape (orchestration engine)
  • GMAKE, BAZEL: Subroutine shape (build tools)
  • NFS, MYSQL: Cylinder shape (storage / database)
  • LLM: Rounded shape (AI/ML component)
  • VERIFY: Diamond shape (decision / verification)
  • T1, T2, T3: Rounded shape (tiered approach)
