### SLIDE 02: THE AI EXECUTION GAP

**Title:** The AI Execution Gap
**Lead Positioning:** Every enterprise knows AI is strategic. Few can move from pilot to production at the speed the market demands. The gap between AI ambition and AI execution is where competitive advantage is won or lost.

**Four Problem Areas:**

1. **POCs That Stall**
   - Promising proofs of concept never reach production
   - Teams prove technical feasibility but lack the infrastructure, data pipelines, or organizational buy-in to scale

2. **Data Chaos**
   - AI initiatives stall at the data layer
   - Fragmented sources, inconsistent formats, and poor data quality block progress before models are even trained

3. **Skills Mismatch**
   - Internal teams understand the domain but lack AI/ML engineering depth
   - External vendors understand AI but not your business or regulatory context

4. **Integration Complexity**
   - AI systems must fit into existing enterprise stacks, not replace them
   - Authentication, compliance, governance, and security requirements add layers of complexity

**Key Statistic:** "Only 12% of enterprises that have deployed AI solutions can effectively execute multi-modal AI capabilities." - Gartner, 2025
