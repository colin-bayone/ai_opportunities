### Slide 06: Enterprise Automation (Most Relevant)

**Lead Positioning:**
> "Move beyond brittle RPA to adaptive intelligent automation. Our agent-based systems integrate with existing enterprise tools and learn from user interactions to continuously improve."

**Four Capability Areas:**

#### 1. Intelligent Process Automation
- AI-powered automation that handles exceptions and adapts to changing inputs
- Replaces rigid rule-based systems with flexible, learning agents
- Natural language interfaces with human-in-the-loop exception handling

**Back-Office Applications:**
- HR: Onboarding workflows, benefits enrollment, policy exceptions
- Finance: Expense approvals, invoice processing, anomaly handling
- Legal: Contract routing, compliance exception handling
- Marketing: Campaign approval workflows, asset management

#### 2. Enterprise Tool Integrations
- Connect AI agents to your enterprise ecosystem through modern standards
- Supports: MCP, Agent-to-Agent, Semantic Kernel, function calling, event-driven architectures
- Integrates with productivity, business, and data systems, plus custom APIs

**Back-Office Applications:**
- HR: Connect to Workday, ADP, ServiceNow
- Finance: Connect to SAP, Oracle, QuickBooks, expense systems
- Legal: Connect to document management, CLM tools
- Marketing: Connect to Salesforce, HubSpot, Adobe

#### 3. Meeting Intelligence
- Automated meeting summarization, action item extraction, and follow-up tracking
- Multi-speaker diarization, context-aware summaries
- CRM integration and automated task creation

**Back-Office Applications:**
- HR: Capture interview notes, performance review discussions
- Finance: Document budget meetings, audit discussions
- Legal: Track negotiation points, contract discussions
- Marketing: Campaign planning meetings, creative reviews

#### 4. Agentic Workflow Systems
- Multi-agent orchestration for complex business processes
- Agents collaborate, delegate, and escalate based on defined policies
- Built on modern agent orchestration frameworks with visual workflow builders

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Foundation**

- Leverage predictive analytics for skills gaps
- Optimize based on data
- Scale without proportionally scaling HR overhead
