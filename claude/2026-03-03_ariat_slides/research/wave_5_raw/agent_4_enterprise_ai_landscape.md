## The AI Implementation Crisis

### Success Rates
- Only **6%** qualify as "AI high performers" (5%+ EBIT impact)
- **70-85%** of AI initiatives fail to meet expected outcomes
- Only **5%** of AI pilots achieve rapid revenue acceleration
- **42%** abandoned most AI initiatives in 2025 (up from 17% in 2024)
- Only **26%** have capabilities to move beyond POC to production

### The Productivity Paradox
- Employees report **40% productivity boost** (self-reported)
- Controlled studies show **25-55% improvements**
- **66%** struggle to establish ROI metrics
- **72%** of AI initiatives actively destroying value
- **69%** of business leaders lost visibility into AI tools
