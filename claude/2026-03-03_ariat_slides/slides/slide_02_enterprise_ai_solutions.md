# Slide 02: Enterprise AI Solutions

**Source:** Topic 2 (Enterprise AI for Back-Office Functions)
**Position:** Shows AI breadth beyond engineering/development

---

## Title
**Enterprise AI Solutions**

## Lead Paragraph
AI applies broadly across the enterprise, not just engineering and development. From recruiting to compliance, finance to marketing, intelligent automation transforms how every department operates.

---

## Visual Layout Concept

**Recommended:** 2x2 capability grid - matches `06_enterprise_automation.html`

Each quadrant represents a department with:
- Inline icon in header
- Department name
- 3-4 bullet capabilities
- Key statistic or case study reference

---

## Content: Four Department Areas

### Card 1: Human Resources
**Icon:** `fa-users`

**Capabilities:**
- Recruiting automation and candidate screening
- AI-powered onboarding workflows
- Policy Q&A assistants
- Attrition prediction and workforce planning

**Proof Point:** Unilever achieved 90% reduction in time-to-hire with 340% ROI in 18 months

---

### Card 2: Finance
**Icon:** `fa-chart-pie`

**Capabilities:**
- Expense processing and anomaly detection
- Invoice automation and AP optimization
- Financial reporting and close acceleration
- Fraud detection and prevention

**Proof Point:** 70% faster AP processing, 200-300% ROI in first year

---

### Card 3: Legal
**Icon:** `fa-scale-balanced`

**Capabilities:**
- Contract review and clause extraction
- Compliance monitoring and alerts
- Document search and knowledge management
- Contract lifecycle management

**Proof Point:** JPMorgan COiN saves $144M annually, reviewing 12,000 agreements in seconds

---

### Card 4: Marketing
**Icon:** `fa-bullhorn`

**Capabilities:**
- Content generation and optimization
- Personalization engines
- Campaign analysis and attribution
- Visual AI for product tagging

**Proof Point:** Fashion retailers see 15-25% conversion lift with AI personalization
