# Opening Section Options

## Option A (Straightforward)

A year ago today I left Coherent Corp after several years building their AI Center of Excellence. It's a 40,000-person global manufacturer with some of the most brilliant engineers I've ever worked with, and I made lifelong friends there. The work was fascinating. Over time, though, things changed, and that change was the signal to open a new chapter.

I came to BayOne Solutions as Director of AI. It was a significant adjustment. I went from a massive enterprise with layers of process to something much smaller and much faster. The people and the culture won me over almost immediately. There was kindness, support, and genuine trust from day one. I came here to build the AI practice, and everyone around me made it clear they were ready to run with me.
