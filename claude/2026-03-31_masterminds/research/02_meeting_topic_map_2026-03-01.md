## Topics Identified

### 1. Group Genesis and Vision
The opening segment where Zac (with Phil) lays out why the group exists, what it is not (not BNI), and the dual purpose: professional development first, referrals as a natural byproduct. Includes initial framing, Phil's additions, and the concept of themed/owned meetings.

### 2. Professional Backgrounds and Practice Deep Dives
Each member's detailed introduction: firm history, practice areas, client types, competitive positioning, firm size, and market niche. This is rich intelligence about what each person actually does and where overlap exists.

### 3. AI in Professional Services
Extended discussion on how AI is being used (or attempted) across law, financial planning, insurance, and consulting. Covers Zox, Lexis Nexis AI, policy review automation, PII concerns, enterprise vs. consumer tools, AI legislation/frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 42001, ISO 27001), and the "fear vs. tool" framing.

### 4. Cross-Discipline Collaboration Opportunities
The substantive discussion on how the group can serve common clients. Business succession planning as a workshop scenario, wealth transfer (boomer retirement), tax code changes (the "one big beautiful bill"), and how each discipline touches the same client differently.

### 5. Mission Statement and Group Structure
The closing segment covering guiding principles, proposed mission language, meeting frequency (monthly), attendance expectations (75-80%), group size (cap around 9-10), potential new members, and the "pride of membership" concept.
