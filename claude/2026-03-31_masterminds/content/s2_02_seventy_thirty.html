How to think about AI in your practice

The 70/30 Reality

All work splits roughly into two buckets. AI is best at absorbing the first so you can spend more time on the second.

70%
Repetitive Work
Monotonous, low-value, and necessary but dull

Document review across contracts, policies, and filings

Data reconciliation and transaction categorization

Report generation and recurring client summaries

Routine correspondence that follows the same structure every time

Renewal and comparison work pulling the same data from different sources

30%
Value-Added Work
What clients actually pay a premium for

Professional judgment in ambiguous situations

Client relationships and trust-building

Creative problem-solving for unique situations

Counsel and empathy during difficult decisions

Strategic thinking across disciplines and situations

AI absorbs the 70% so you can spend more time on the 30%. The professionals who benefit most from AI are the ones who use it to clear the repetitive work off their plate and redirect that time toward the work their clients actually value.
