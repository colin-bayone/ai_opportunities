Project Overview

This transcript is from BayOne Solutions' engagement with Sephora regarding their enterprise data warehouse modernization program. BayOne is positioning to expand from a staffing relationship to a strategic AI and data engineering partnership.

Sephora is undertaking a 3-year EDW modernization from 2026-2028, migrating from SQL Server, Cognos, and DataStage to Databricks, Tableau, and ThoughtSpot. The scope includes approximately 6,000 reports, 8 SSAS cubes, and 800+ KPIs to migrate.

BayOne has an existing staffing relationship with Sephora and is seeking to add a solutions track focused on AI-assisted migration acceleration.
