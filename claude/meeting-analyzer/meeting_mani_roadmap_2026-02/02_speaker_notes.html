"We're starting a big project called EDW Modernization. This project is to move from all legacy technologies into modern technologies."

"We're going to be re-engineering all the reports that we have done over the past 15, 20 years. Over the past 15, 20 years, we have about seven, like thousands of reports, basically."

"Right now the plan is already going till 2028. But we're trying to see how we can possibly do it in 2027 or maybe finish it off by early 2028."

On AI Acceleration Needs

"If we go report by report, it will take a long time for us. So we are saying, what would be an easier way, what would be the right way, like efficient way to do this. Instead of going report by report, can we do something like maybe if we do this, we can finish off three reports at one shot. We can finish off like six reports at one shot, so we can expedite the delivery."

"Those things we are looking for AI to come and help to say, okay, look at the codebase, look at things, see actually, come up with, AI would itself proactively come and tell us, like these things are something to watch out for. This is what you can take. So it will tell us where all actually, it will analyze the codebase and then say, okay, these are all the places for us to mind for."
