# Context Discovery - Mani Roadmap Meeting

## Project Overview

This transcript is from **BayOne Solutions' engagement with Sephora** regarding their enterprise data warehouse (EDW) modernization program. BayOne is positioning to expand from a staffing relationship to a strategic AI and data engineering partnership.

**Key engagement context:**
- Sephora is undertaking a **3-year EDW modernization** (2026-2028)
- Migration from SQL Server/Cognos/DataStage to Databricks/Tableau/ThoughtSpot
- ~6,000 reports, 8 SSAS cubes, 800+ KPIs to migrate
- BayOne has existing staffing relationship; seeking to add solutions track
