McGrath Support Proposal

Prerequisites

Items required from McGrath to ensure successful service delivery and transition

System Access & Environments
Provisioned access to Salesforce (Prod, Sandbox/UAT), CPQ, integration platforms, monitoring/logging tools, and ticketing systems
Access to ticket history, incident reports, release notes, and known issue logs for baseline assessment
Access to product catalog, pricing rules, approval workflows, discount logic, and custom scripts within CPQ
Production replica environment available to simulate and recreate production issues
Documentation & Data
Availability of current solution architecture, data model, integration inventory, and CPQ configuration details
Documentation of all real-time and batch integrations, including endpoints, error handling, and dependencies
Sample datasets and reports to assess data quality, duplicates, and data integrity
People, Process & Governance
Named SPOCs from Business, IT, and Operations for discovery, validation, and ongoing clarifications
Defined roles, access controls, audit requirements, and compliance guidelines
Established release, deployment, and rollback processes (or alignment to define them)
Access and escalation coordination with third-party vendors supporting integrations or add-ons
Structured knowledge transfer sessions and handover documentation from incumbent/implementation team
Prerequisites will be reviewed and confirmed during the on-boarding and discovery phase
