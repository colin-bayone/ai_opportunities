Case Study

Accelerating Reporting for Oracle Fusion ERP

Global Manufacturing Enterprise: Custom BI Publisher reporting across Finance and Supply Chain

Business Scenario

Global manufacturing enterprise implemented Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP across Finance and Supply Chain. Stakeholders required custom reports for reconciliation, regulatory reporting, and operational decision making.

Limited visibility into supplier liabilities and customer receivables
Finance teams relying on manual Excel reconciliation
Integration reporting required for CRM and tax platforms
Technical Architecture
ERP Data BIP Data Model (SQL) RTF Templates Automated Bursting
Automated report scheduling
Email bursting to stakeholders
Integration extracts for downstream systems
Finance reconciliation dashboards

Solution Approach

25+ custom BI Publisher reports across Finance and SCM modules
Custom BIP data models and SQL queries for complex reporting needs
Business-friendly RTF layouts with automated bursting and email distribution
Integration extracts for downstream systems across business units
Finance: AP Aging, AR, Trial Balance
Procurement: POs, Supplier Spend
Inventory: Stock on Hand, Valuation
Order Mgmt: Status, Fulfillment

Business Outcomes

40% reduction in manual reconciliation effort
Real-time financial visibility across all business units
Faster month-end closing cycles through automated reporting
Improved supplier and customer reporting transparency
Seamless integration reporting with SCM, CRM, and analytics tools
