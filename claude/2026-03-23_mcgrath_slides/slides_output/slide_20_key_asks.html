McGrath Support Proposal

Key Asks

What BayOne needs from McGrath to ensure a successful engagement

Knowledge Transfer Facilitation

Designate internal stakeholders to participate in structured KT sessions. Ensure documentation, runbooks, and tribal knowledge from the incumbent vendor are accessible to the BayOne team during the transition window.

Environment & Infrastructure Access

Provision environment access, system credentials, VPN connectivity, and tooling licenses within the first two weeks of engagement. Assign infrastructure team contacts for access requests and escalations.

Timely Information Sharing

Coordinate with the incumbent vendor to ensure operational documentation, integration specifications, and support history are handed off on schedule. Identify a McGrath liaison to unblock information requests.

Approvals & Decision Cadence

Establish a streamlined approval workflow for entry/exit criteria, software procurement, and access provisioning. Commit to defined turnaround times for sign-offs to keep the transition timeline on track.
