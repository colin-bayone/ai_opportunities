One-pager executive summary of the Zeblok partnership meeting for internal distribution and potential external sharing.

## Approach (Per User Direction)

1. **Phase 1:** Draft as plain Markdown without heavy formatting

- BayOne leadership (Rahul, Yogesh - founders)

- Outcome

### About Zeblok (Brief)
- Who they are
- What they do
- Why they matter

### Partnership Model

- Bay One Innovation Lab proposal

### Agreed Next Steps
- Immediate actions
- Target milestone (GTC)

### Market Opportunities
- Primary: Retail
- Secondary: Defense
- Emerging: Education, QE

### Why This Matters for BayOne
- Differentiation from staffing
- Defense market entry
- AI infrastructure capability

## Key Messages to Convey

1. **Alignment:** Both parties see clear value in partnership
2. **Urgency:** GTC timeline creates momentum
3. **Concrete:** Not vague interest - specific next steps agreed
4. **Strategic:** Positions BayOne beyond staffing
5. **Defense:** Real opportunity with real relationships (clearances, generals)

## What to Emphasize

- Mouli's credibility (JP Morgan, Wall Street, HPC background)
- Platform differentiation (GPU reduction, air-gapped, PQC)
- Defense connections (fund, cleared personnel, general access)
- BayOne fit (retail accounts, US Persons, Colin's background)

## What to Downplay/Omit

- Zeblok's small size (under 20 people)
- Revenue estimates (<$5M)
- Open compliance questions
- Technical claims not yet validated
