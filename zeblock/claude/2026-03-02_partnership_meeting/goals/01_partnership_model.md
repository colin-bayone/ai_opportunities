# Zeblok Labs Partnership Model

## Core Concept

Mouli's framing:
> "Whoever works with us, there has to be a willingness to participate via a Zeblok Labs mechanism. Because that's where it gets real."

Zeblok Labs is Mouli's **litmus test** for partnership seriousness.

---

## What is Zeblok Labs?

A co-innovation framework where:
- Developers from both sides are actively involved
- Market-ready solutions are tailored for specific industries
- Real integration work happens (not just sales partnerships)
- Technology validation occurs before customer deployment

### Existing Zeblok Labs Instance
- **Location:** Stony Brook University
- **Assets:** 60-70 GPUs under management
- **Users:** Faculty executing SBIR/STTR grants
- **Limitation:** Doesn't have newest generation hardware yet

## Zeblok Verse Concept

Suraj's interpretation:
> "Creating a marketplace for BayOne... BayOne Verse. We will have for industries, agentic apps"

Each Innovation Lab partner creates their own "verse":
- Industry-specific agentic apps
- Reusable assets ("so we don't reinvent every time")
- Pre-integrated ISV solutions

### Existing Zeblok Verse Components
- Agentic learning systems (education ISV)
- Banking/insurance agents (FinTech ISV)
- AI-native development/testing (QE ISV)
- Sensor fusion/recognition (defense ISV)
- Simple AI (Manish's agent store) - being aggregated

## Operating Principles

### From Mouli

1. **Focus** - "Better to keep focus on one market segment... execute the hell out of it"

2. **Concrete starting point** - "We need to work with something concrete to identify go-to-market opportunities"

3. **Velocity matters** - "For me, it's the velocity of execution"

4. **Skills validation** - Innovation lab validates "that skills exist, there's ability to execute on both sides"

5. **Not just tech demo** - "This is not just a tech company showing off tech stuff. There's a real business."

### Success Indicators
- Active engagement in lab activities
- Market-ready solutions developed
- Customer deployments from lab work
- Defense opportunities flow (if qualified)
