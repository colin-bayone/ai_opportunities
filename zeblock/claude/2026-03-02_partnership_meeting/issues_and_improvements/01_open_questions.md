# Open Questions and Items to Probe

## Critical Compliance Questions (From Briefing - Not Yet Asked)

These questions were prepared but not covered in the meeting. Should be addressed in follow-up:

### 1. FIPS 140-2/140-3 Validation
> "Are the cryptographic modules in Ai-MicroCloud FIPS 140-2 or 140-3 validated?"

**Why it matters:** Hard requirement for CUI handling. Not enough to "use AES-256" - need CMVP certificate number.

### 2. NIST 800-171 / SP 800-53 Mapping
> "Have you mapped Ai-MicroCloud against NIST 800-171 or SP 800-53 controls?"

**Why it matters:** Defense contractors' assessors will scrutinize all software in scope.

### 3. Shared Responsibility Matrix
> "Can you provide a shared responsibility matrix showing which CMMC controls Ai-MicroCloud satisfies vs. customer responsibility?"

**Why it matters:** Critical for positioning to defense customers.

### 4. Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)
> "Can you produce an SBOM for Ai-MicroCloud?"

**Why it matters:** Supply chain security increasingly required.

### 5. Third-Party Security Assessments
> "Have you had any third-party security assessments or penetration tests?"

**Why it matters:** Enterprise and defense customers will ask.

### 6. US Persons on Engineering Team
> "What percentage of your engineering team are U.S. Persons?"

**Why it matters:** ITAR requires US Persons for any access to controlled data. Zeblok has India subsidiary.

### 7. STIG Compatibility
> "Can Ai-MicroCloud run on STIG-hardened infrastructure?"

**Why it matters:** DoD environments require DISA STIG compliance.

### 8. Defense Deployments
> "Have any defense contractors or defense primes deployed your platform today?"

**Why it matters:** Reference customers would validate claims.
