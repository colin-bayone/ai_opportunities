# Document Critique: Zeblok Partnership Executive Summary v3

## Overall Assessment

**Verdict: NEEDS REVISION**

The document is substantively strong with clear analysis and well-structured arguments. However, several anti-patterns and professional standards issues require correction before it meets Big Four quality standards.

---

## Issues by Category

### 1. First Person Voice (Anti-Pattern #5)

The document uses "we" extensively instead of organizational voice.

| Line | Issue | Fix |
|------|-------|-----|
| 11 | "We met with Mouli Narayanan" | "BayOne met with Mouli Narayanan" |
| 50 | "our talent engine for US Persons" | "BayOne's talent engine for US Persons" |
| 125 | "We bring delivery capacity" | "BayOne brings delivery capacity" |
| 157 | "We need a technology story" | "BayOne needs a technology story" |

**Recommendation:** Replace all instances of "we," "our," and "us" with "BayOne" or equivalent organizational voice.

### 2. Colloquial Language (Anti-Pattern #7)

Several phrases are too casual for a professional document.

| Line | Issue | Fix |

| 13 | "clear eyes on where the fit actually is" | "realistic assessment of where the opportunity lies" |
| 159 | "The value is real, and it's mutual." | "The partnership offers mutual value." |
