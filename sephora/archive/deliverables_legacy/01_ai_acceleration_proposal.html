The legacy EDW represents 15-20 years of accumulated business logic, with queries buried in old Cognos reports and thousands of DataStage ETL pipelines. Manual review of each component creates a multi-year timeline that delays business value realization.

Core bottleneck: Report-by-report migration is too slow. The current approach requires manual analysis, mapping validation, and re-engineering for each of the 6,000 reports, creating a timeline that stretches through 2028.

02

Our Approach: AI-Assisted Acceleration

We propose an AI-assisted methodology that shifts from individual report migration to pattern-based batch processing, reducing manual review through intelligent automation.

Pattern Detection and Clustering

Rather than treating each report as unique, our approach identifies common structures across the 6,000-report portfolio. Reports sharing similar schemas, query patterns, or business logic can be grouped and migrated together, with AI generating migration templates validated once per cluster rather than per report.

Intelligent Codebase Analysis

AI scans legacy Cognos and SQL code to surface buried dependencies, hidden business logic, and potential migration risks before they become blockers. This proactive identification replaces reactive discovery during execution, reducing rework and timeline surprises.

Automated Mapping and Validation

Current mapping validation remains heavily manual despite AI-assisted suggestions. We focus on reducing the last mile, using AI to validate mappings against historical patterns, flag anomalies for human review, and auto-approve low-risk transformations that match established templates.
