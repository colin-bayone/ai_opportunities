- **Colin as differentiator.** Both the technical presentation and the screening capability were positioned as BayOne advantages. Vaibhav was clearly engaged with Colin's approach
- **Rate flexibility.** The $120/hr rate for Deepika's role has some flexibility. Vaibhav acknowledged rates are based on legacy QE cards, not current AI market rates. He would need VP approval for higher rates but can argue for it if there is demonstrated value
