Sephora EDW Modernization – Meeting Recap
1. Current State of Sephora’s EDW Modernization

	•	Sephora is in the middle of a three-year EDW modernization program.

	•	This is NOT a lift-and-shift migration — it is a full re-engineering initiative.

	•	Goals:

	•	Move from legacy on-prem Cognos + SSAS to Databricks.

	•	Rebuild reporting architecture → retain Cognos front-end for now to reduce change-management friction.

	•	Evaluate transition over time to ThoughtSpot/Tableau, but not a priority now.

	•	Pipelines are being moved from DataStage → Databricks pipelines (native or other accelerator-based approaches).

	•	Semantic layer work is happening gradually.

	•	Team will not allow semantic modeling to slow the program.

	•	Will define terminology where it adds value but not as a blocker.

2. Complexity & Pain Points Identified

	•	Thousands of existing Cognos reports; tribal knowledge buried across multiple teams.

	•	Challenge: re-engineering all logic without losing functionality users rely on.

	•	Ensuring parity during the transition is critical: definitions, KPIs, validations.

	•	Internal SMEs are strong but bandwidth-constrained—they are needed for day-to-day production support, not just modernization.
