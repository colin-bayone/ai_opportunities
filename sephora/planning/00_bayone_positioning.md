## Risks to Navigate

### Decentralization Complexity
- Reporting is being embedded into domain teams (Stores, E-commerce, Supply Chain)
- Multiple stakeholders emerging
- May need to work across organizational boundaries
- **Opportunity:** Help with governance framework as Mani's team transitions

### Scope Creep
- 3-year program is large
- Need clear boundaries on what we own vs. what Sephora owns
- Phased approach helps manage scope

### The SSAS Cube Trap
- High-risk, high-reward area
- If no one can force change on business users, building compatibility shims will be painful
- Should be eyes-open about whether this is winnable
