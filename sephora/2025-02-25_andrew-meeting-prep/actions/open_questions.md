## Question Strategy

### Opening
Start with broad question to understand their perspective:
> "How do you see AI acceleration fitting into the modernization program?"

### Deepening
Follow up based on response:
> "Where do you see the biggest gaps that AI could fill?"

### Specific
Get actionable details:
> "What would a good pilot look like from your perspective?"

### Competitive
Gather intelligence carefully:
> "How does Databricks fit into the AI acceleration conversation?"

### Close
Concrete next steps:
> "Would it be possible to get sample reports to make our proposal more concrete?"
