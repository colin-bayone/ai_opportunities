## Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### For Pilot (Option 1)
- Consider loss-leader pricing to prove value
- Fixed price with clear deliverables
- Low enough to be approved quickly
- Demonstrate ROI for larger engagement

### For Co-Delivery (Option 2)
- T&M with caps
- Blended rates (mix of levels)
- Clear scope boundaries
- Milestone-based payments

### For Partnership (Option 3)
- Outcome-based components
- Volume discounts for commitment
- Flexibility premium for rotating specialists
- Long-term relationship pricing
