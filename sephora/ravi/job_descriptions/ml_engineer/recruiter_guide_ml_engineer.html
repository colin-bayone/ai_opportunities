Recruiter Guide: Machine Learning Engineer

Remote (Offshore, India preferred) | Experience: 3-7 years | Azure only

Role Summary

MLOps-focused role for someone who can develop, deploy, and operate machine learning models in production. Hands-on, confident, comfortable across the full model lifecycle. This is NOT a GenAI/chatbot role.

Hard Requirement: AI pair programming tools (Claude Code, Cursor, or Windsurf). GitHub Copilot is NOT sufficient. Must ask about this in screening. Candidates should discuss specific benefits and weaknesses of their tool, not generic answers.

What to Look For

Strong Signals

  • MLOps, model deployment, model serving
  • Production ML systems at scale
  • Azure ML or Azure cloud experience
  • PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras in production
  • Feature stores, model monitoring
  • ONNX, TF Serving, or MLEAP (great fit)
  • CI/CD for ML pipelines
  • Blend of MLOps with some LLM mentions

Weak Signals (De-prioritize)

  • Only chatbot or conversational AI
  • Only research or academic ML
  • AWS-only or on-prem-only experience
  • Pure data science without deployment
  • Cannot discuss production deployments
  • Dismissive of AI pair programming tools
  • Cannot explain end-to-end ML lifecycle

Screening Questions

  • "Walk me through the last model you deployed to production. What was the architecture? How did you handle serving?"
  • "What AI pair programming tools do you use? Walk me through your typical workflow with [tool]. What are its strengths and weaknesses?"
  • "What is your experience with Azure ML? If not Azure specifically, what cloud ML platforms have you used?"
  • "Have you worked with LLMs in any of your projects? How did you integrate them?" (Good: practical use as part of larger system. Concerning: dismissive or only theoretical)

Key Points

  • This is MLOps, not GenAI. Looking for engineers who use language models effectively, not chatbot builders.
  • Production experience is non-negotiable. Rare for someone under 3 years to have this.
  • Azure only. Reject AWS-only or on-prem-only candidates.
  • Should be confident and energetic when discussing their work.
  • Chatbot experience is not disqualifying but should be de-prioritized.
  • Many ML professionals are uncomfortable with LLMs. Screen for this.
