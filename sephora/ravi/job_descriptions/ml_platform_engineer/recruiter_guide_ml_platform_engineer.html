Recruiter Guide: ML Platform Engineer

Remote (Offshore, India preferred) | Experience: 5-8 years | Azure only

Role Summary

Senior full-stack ML professional who owns both model development AND data infrastructure. This is a rare profile combining deep MLOps expertise with strong data engineering skills. Expect difficulty sourcing. These candidates should demonstrate end-to-end ownership across the entire ML stack.

Hard Requirement: AI pair programming tools (Claude Code, Cursor, or Windsurf). GitHub Copilot is NOT sufficient. Must ask about this in screening. Candidates should discuss specific benefits and weaknesses of their tool, not generic answers.

What to Look For

Strong Signals

  • MLOps AND data pipeline experience
  • Model deployment, serving, monitoring
  • Spark, Kafka, Databricks proficiency
  • Azure ML and Azure data platform
  • Feature stores (build and operate)
  • ONNX, TF Serving, or MLEAP
  • Kubernetes for ML deployments
  • CI/CD for both models and data
  • Scala experience (Spark context)
  • Architecture and system design skills

Weak Signals (De-prioritize)

  • Only ML, no data engineering
  • Only data engineering, no ML
  • AWS-only or on-prem-only experience
  • Admin or IT-focused background
  • Only research or academic ML
  • Cannot discuss end-to-end systems
  • Dismissive of AI pair programming tools
  • Primarily chatbot or GenAI focus

Screening Questions

  • "Describe a system where you owned both the model and the data infrastructure. How did they connect?"
  • "What AI pair programming tools do you use? Walk me through your typical workflow. What are its strengths and weaknesses?"
  • "Tell me about your experience with Spark. Have you used it for both ETL and model training?"
  • "How do you approach CI/CD for ML systems? What about for data pipelines?"
  • "What is your experience with Azure's ML and data platforms?"

Key Points

  • This is a rare profile. Expect difficulty sourcing and be prepared to cast a wider net.
  • Must demonstrate BOTH ML and data engineering depth. One without the other is insufficient.
  • End-to-end ownership mentality is critical. They should want to own the full stack.
  • Azure only. Reject AWS-only or on-prem-only candidates.
  • Higher experience bar: 5+ years software engineering, 3+ years ML, 2+ years data engineering.
  • Should be highly confident and able to draw system architecture on the spot.
