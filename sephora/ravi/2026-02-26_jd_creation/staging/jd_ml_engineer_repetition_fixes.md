# ML Engineer JD - Repetition Fixes

**Problem:** "ML" appears 30+ times in the document. Reads as repetitive and keyword-stuffed.

**Strategy:**
1. Spell out "machine learning" in the title/overview to establish context
2. Keep "ML" in section headers
3. After establishing context, drop the qualifier - it's obvious this is an ML role

---

## Proposed Changes

### Title
**Current:** `# ML Engineer`
**Proposed:** `# Machine Learning Engineer`
**Rationale:** Spell it out once in the title, then abbreviate elsewhere
