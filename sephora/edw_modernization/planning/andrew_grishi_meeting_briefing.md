## Things to Avoid

### Don't Say
- "Migration" (it's "re-engineering" or "modernization")
- "Replace" existing tools (complement, not replace)
- "AI will solve everything" (be realistic)
- "We're better than Databricks" (complement them)
- Generic consulting speak (be specific to their situation)

### Don't Do
- Talk more than listen
- Oversell capabilities
- Ignore either person (balance attention)
- Make commitments you can't keep
- Skip the staffing track entirely
